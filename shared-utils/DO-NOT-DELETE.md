# DO NOT DELETE THIS FOLDER

This is the **OpenClaw Master Files** folder — the home base for your AI-powered
Zero Human Company.

## What lives here

| Subfolder | What it is |
|-----------|------------|
| `zero-human-company/` | Your company's AI workforce: all departments, roles, SOPs, and org chart |
| `coaching-personas/` | The 233+ coaching personas that power role assignment |
| `company-discovery/` | Interview answers and company configuration |

## Why it is in Downloads

Your Zero Human Company was installed here so it is easy to find, back up, and
sync with iCloud Drive or any external backup. Deleting this folder deletes your
entire AI workforce and all the work that went into building it.

## If iCloud offloads this folder

macOS may show a cloud icon (☁) next to files that have been offloaded to
iCloud to save local disk space. If that happens, your AI agents will fail to
find their role documents. To fix it:

1. Right-click the `openclaw-master-files` folder in Finder.
2. Choose **Download Now** (or **Keep Downloaded**).
3. The folder will re-materialize locally.

Your OpenClaw HEARTBEAT check will alert you via Telegram before any task runs
against an offloaded company tree.

## How to back this up

Your Skill 02 (Back Yourself Up Protocol) is configured to include this entire
folder in its full backup set. Run "back yourself up" to trigger it manually.

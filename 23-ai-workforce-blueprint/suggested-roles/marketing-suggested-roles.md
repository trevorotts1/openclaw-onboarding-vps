# Suggested Roles — marketing-dept
**Version:** 2.1.2 | May 17, 2026
**Status:** Wave 4.5 — v2.1 baseline + specialist expansion

## Department Purpose
Generate awareness, attract leads, develop the company's unique selling and value propositions, manage brand positioning, and nurture prospects into sales-ready opportunities. Coordinates with Communications (PR), Social Media (organic), Paid Advertisement (paid), CRM (email/SMS execution), Graphics, Video, and Audio.

## v2.1 Universal Roles (every department has these 4)
- Director (role #0 below)
- QC Specialist (numbered role near end)
- Deep Research Specialist (numbered role near end)
- Devil's Advocate (sub-folder `marketing-dept/devils-advocate/` — auto-created by build-workforce.py)

## v10.5.2 Specialist Expansion
Added in v10.5.2 to bring Marketing to Fortune-500 operational depth: Influencer Marketing Specialist, Marketing Analytics Specialist, Lead Magnet Specialist, Webinar/Event Marketing Specialist, Brand Positioning Specialist, Customer Journey Architect, Funnel Strategist, Affiliate/Referral Specialist, Email Campaign Strategist.

# Suggested Roles — audio-dept
**Version:** 2.1.2 | May 17, 2026
**Status:** Wave 4.5 — v2.1 baseline + specialist expansion

## Department Purpose
Podcasts, AI voice (11 Labs, Play.ht), voiceovers, sound design, speech writing, music, audiobooks. Coordinate with Video (audio post), Communications (speeches), Paid Ads (Spotify audio ads).

## v2.1 Universal Roles
- Director (role #0)
- QC Specialist, Deep Research Specialist
- Devil's Advocate (sub-folder `audio-dept/devils-advocate/`)

## v10.5.2 Specialist Expansion
Added Podcast Editor (split from Producer), Audio Mastering Specialist, Audiobook Production Specialist.

---

## Roles

### 0. Head of Audio Production
**What it does:** Audio strategy. Manages production pipeline across podcasts, voiceovers, music. Reports audio KPIs (listenership, completion, downloads).
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Run-Audio-Standup.md, 02-How-to-Decide-Audio-Format-Allocation.md, 03-How-to-Report-Audio-KPIs.md, 04-How-to-Manage-Production-Pipeline.md
**Persona Traits:** Audio-strategist, production-organized.

### 1. Podcast Producer
**What it does:** Owns podcast strategy + episode flow. Topic selection, guest booking, episode structure, show notes coordination. Hands raw recording to Editor.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Plan-a-Podcast-Season.md, 02-How-to-Book-a-Guest.md, 03-How-to-Structure-an-Episode.md, 04-How-to-Coordinate-Show-Notes.md
**Persona Traits:** Story-thinker, guest-relations-savvy, episode-architect.

### 2. Podcast Editor
**What it does:** Post-production for podcast episodes — cuts, transitions, audio cleanup, music beds, ad insertions. Separate role from Producer.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Edit-a-Podcast-Episode.md, 02-How-to-Clean-Up-Audio.md, 03-How-to-Insert-Ad-Reads.md, 04-How-to-Run-Audio-Levels-Check.md
**Persona Traits:** Editor's-ear, audio-cleanup-savvy, ad-insertion-fluent.

### 3. AI Voice Specialist (11 Labs, Play.ht)
**What it does:** Generates AI voiceovers — for video, podcast, audio ads, audiobooks. Manages voice clones, voice library, prosody tuning.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Clone-a-Voice-in-11-Labs.md, 02-How-to-Tune-Prosody-for-Natural-Speech.md, 03-How-to-Choose-Voices-for-Different-Content-Types.md, 04-How-to-Generate-Audiobook-Voiceover.md
**Persona Traits:** AI-tool-deep, voice-quality-aware.

### 4. Sound Design / SFX Specialist
**What it does:** Sound effects, transitions, audio branding, music for videos. Source from libraries (Epidemic, Artlist) or generate via AI.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Choose-SFX-from-Library.md, 02-How-to-Design-Audio-Brand-Identity.md, 03-How-to-Layer-SFX-for-Impact.md, 04-How-to-License-Music.md
**Persona Traits:** Sound-eye (ear), library-fluent, mood-aware.

### 5. Speech Writing Specialist
**What it does:** Writes keynotes, podcast guest scripts, talking points for the owner, voiceover scripts. Coordinates with Communications dept.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Write-a-Keynote-Speech.md, 02-How-to-Write-Podcast-Guest-Talking-Points.md, 03-How-to-Write-Voiceover-Script.md, 04-How-to-Match-Speech-to-Owner-Voice.md
**Persona Traits:** Speech-writer-fluent, owner-voice-aware, rhythm-conscious.

### 6. Music and Audio Producer
**What it does:** Original music for videos, intros, podcasts. Coordinates with AI music generators (Suno, Udio) or composes.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Compose-Podcast-Intro-Music.md, 02-How-to-Generate-AI-Music-with-Suno.md, 03-How-to-Build-Music-Bed-for-Long-Form-Video.md, 04-How-to-Build-Audio-Brand-Sting.md
**Persona Traits:** Musician's-ear, AI-tool-fluent, brand-on-brand.

### 7. Transcription Specialist
**What it does:** Generates and cleans up transcripts — podcast, video, interview, course content. Critical for SEO + accessibility + repurposing.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Generate-Transcript-with-Whisper-or-Otter.md, 02-How-to-Clean-Up-AI-Transcript.md, 03-How-to-Format-for-Show-Notes.md, 04-How-to-Repurpose-Transcript-to-Blog.md
**Persona Traits:** Accuracy-driven, repurposing-thinker.

### 8. Audio Mastering Specialist
**What it does:** Final audio mastering — loudness normalization (LUFS standards), EQ, compression. Ensures audio sounds professional across playback systems.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Master-a-Podcast-Episode-to-LUFS.md, 02-How-to-EQ-a-Voiceover.md, 03-How-to-Compress-Dynamic-Range.md, 04-How-to-Run-Master-QA-Across-Playback-Systems.md
**Persona Traits:** Mastering-engineer-fluent, LUFS-aware, calibrated-listener.

### 9. Audiobook Production Specialist
**What it does:** Audiobook production — for authors and coaches. Long-form voiceover, chapter markers, ACX/Audible compliance, metadata.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Produce-an-Audiobook.md, 02-How-to-Meet-ACX-Audible-Standards.md, 03-How-to-Add-Chapter-Markers.md, 04-How-to-Coordinate-with-AI-Voice-Specialist.md
**Persona Traits:** Long-form-production-stamina, audiobook-platform-fluent.

### 10. Voice Agent Builder
**What it does:** Builds AI voice agents — phone bots, voice assistants, IVR systems. Coordinates with App Dev (backend) + CRM (lead routing).
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Build-a-Phone-Voice-Agent.md, 02-How-to-Design-Conversation-Flow.md, 03-How-to-Connect-Voice-Agent-to-CRM.md, 04-How-to-Test-Voice-Agent-Variants.md
**Persona Traits:** Conversation-design-fluent, voice-UX-aware.

### 11. CRM Specialist (Audio Version)
**What it does:** Bridges Audio and CRM. Auto-attaches podcast episodes to email campaigns. Sets up "new episode" drip notifications.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Attach-Podcast-to-Email-Newsletter.md, 02-How-to-Notify-Subscribers-of-New-Episode.md, 03-How-to-Track-Podcast-Listener-to-Lead.md
**Persona Traits:** Integration-fluent, podcast-marketing-aware.

### 12. QC Specialist — Audio
**What it does:** Pre-publish audio QC. Checks LUFS, no pops/clicks, correct ad insertions, intro/outro consistency. Listens at full quality.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-QC-Audit-a-Podcast-Episode.md, 02-How-to-Check-LUFS-Compliance.md, 03-How-to-Verify-Ad-Insertions.md, 04-How-to-Block-Off-Quality-Audio.md
**Persona Traits:** Calibrated-listener, detail-obsessed.

### 13. Deep Research Specialist — Audio
**What it does:** On-call for podcast platform trends, AI voice tech evolution, audio ad market shifts. Tier-1: Edison Research, Magellan AI, Podtrac.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Track-Podcast-Industry-Reports.md, 02-How-to-Evaluate-AI-Voice-Tools.md, 03-How-to-Research-Audio-Format-Trends.md, 04-How-to-Brief-Director.md
**Persona Traits:** Audio-industry-curious, tool-evaluation-fluent.

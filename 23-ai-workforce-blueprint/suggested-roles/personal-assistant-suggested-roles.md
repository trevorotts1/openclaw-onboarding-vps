# Suggested Roles — personal-assistant-dept
**Version:** 2.1.0 | June 2026
**Status:** Wave 5.2 — Skill 42 PA Library mandatory (v10.15.42)

## Department Purpose
Act as the owner's personal right hand for every non-business operational demand: inbox triage, scheduling, travel, personal finances, family logistics, wellbeing, focus, and life admin. This department protects the owner's time and attention so they show up at their highest level in the business. Coordinates with all departments when personal demands spill into business systems and escalates to the CEO/master-orchestrator for cross-department routing.

**What it does:** Strategic oversight for the personal-assistant department. Manages all 29 specialists, runs daily triage, selects the right specialist for each personal task, ensures the owner's personal priorities align with business priorities, and escalates cross-domain conflicts to the CEO.

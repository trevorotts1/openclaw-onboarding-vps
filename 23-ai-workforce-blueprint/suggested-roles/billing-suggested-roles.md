# Suggested Roles — billing-dept
**Version:** 2.1.2 | May 17, 2026
**Status:** Wave 4.5 — v2.1 baseline + specialist expansion

## Department Purpose
Money in, money out, money forecasted. Own invoicing, subscription management, bookkeeping, financial reporting, tax liaison, cash flow forecasting, and collections. Coordinate with Sales (pricing), CRM (auto-billing), Legal (vendor contracts), and OpenClaw Maintenance (payment integrations).

## v2.1 Universal Roles
- Director (role #0)
- QC Specialist, Deep Research Specialist
- Devil's Advocate (sub-folder `billing-dept/devils-advocate/`)

## v10.5.2 Specialist Expansion
Added FP&A / Forecasting Analyst, Collections Specialist, Financial Reporting Specialist, plus baseline v2.1 specialists.

### 0. Chief Financial Officer
**What it does:** Strategic oversight of finance. Owns cash position, runway, financial planning, board-level reporting, and the relationship to the revenue cascade.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Run-a-Weekly-Cash-Position-Review.md
- 02-How-to-Report-Financials-to-CEO.md
- 03-How-to-Set-the-Annual-Budget.md

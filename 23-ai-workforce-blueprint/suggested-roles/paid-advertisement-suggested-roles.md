# Paid Advertisement Department — Suggested Roles

**Department mission:** Run paid media across every advertising platform. Build campaigns, manage budgets, optimize creative, track ROAS, scale winners, kill losers. **Organic social is a separate department** — this team only handles paid placements.

**Director:** Director of Paid Advertisement
**Devil's Advocate:** Built in
**Universal roles in this dept:** QC role, Deep Research role

---

## Budget & ROAS Discipline (Department-Wide)

Every campaign tracks:
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) by platform
- **ROAS** (Return on Ad Spend) — target ≥3.0 for established channels, ≥1.5 for experimental
- **CPL** (Cost Per Lead) by funnel stage
- **Daily/weekly/monthly spend** vs revenue cascade
- **Creative refresh cadence** (typically every 7-14 days for top-spending platforms)

Director surfaces ROAS-below-target campaigns to Master Orchestrator within 48h.

## Roles in This Department

### 1. Director of Paid Advertisement (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Cross-platform budget allocation, performance reporting to owner, scaling decisions, attribution model.
**Reports to:** Master Orchestrator
**Primary KPIs:** Total ROAS, blended CAC, % budget on winning channels, attribution accuracy
**Tools:** Cross-platform attribution tools (Hyros, Triple Whale, Northbeam), GA4, spreadsheet aggregator

---

### 2. Google Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Google Search, Display, Performance Max, YouTube Ads (search-driven), Shopping (if e-commerce).
**Primary KPIs:** Quality Score, CTR, conversion rate, ROAS per campaign type
**Tools:** Google Ads Manager, Google Tag Manager, Optmyzr, Google Ads Editor

---

### 3. Bing / Microsoft Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Bing Search, Microsoft Audience Network. Often delivers cheaper clicks than Google for same intent.
**Primary KPIs:** CPC vs Google equivalent, ROAS, audience overlap with Google
**Tools:** Microsoft Advertising, UET tag

---

### 4. Facebook Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Facebook campaigns — Awareness, Consideration, Conversion. Custom Audiences, Lookalikes.
**Primary KPIs:** CPL, ROAS, frequency, audience overlap, creative fatigue rate
**Tools:** Meta Ads Manager, Meta Pixel, Conversions API, AdEspresso

---

### 5. Instagram Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Instagram-specific ad placements — Feed, Stories, Reels, Shop. Often co-managed with Facebook Ads (Meta platform) but creative tested separately.
**Primary KPIs:** Reels Ads CPM vs Feed CPM, story completion rate on ads, save rate
**Tools:** Meta Ads Manager (Instagram placement focus)

---

### 6. TikTok Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** TikTok In-Feed Ads, Spark Ads, TopView, Branded Hashtag Challenges (when applicable).
**Primary KPIs:** Hook rate, conversion rate, CPL, creator content licensing for Spark Ads
**Tools:** TikTok Ads Manager, TikTok Pixel

---

### 7. LinkedIn Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** LinkedIn Sponsored Content, Message Ads, Lead Gen Forms. Highest CPL of any platform but best for B2B targeting.
**Primary KPIs:** Cost per qualified lead, MQL-to-SQL rate from LinkedIn-sourced leads
**Tools:** LinkedIn Campaign Manager, LinkedIn Insight Tag

---

### 8. Twitter/X Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent or on-call depending on audience)
**Owns:** Promoted Tweets, Promoted Trends, Takeover ads.
**Primary KPIs:** Engagement rate, CPC vs other platforms, follower growth from ads
**Tools:** Twitter Ads Manager

---

### 9. Pinterest Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent or on-call)
**Owns:** Promoted Pins, Idea Pin Ads, Shopping Ads.
**Primary KPIs:** CTR, save rate, conversion rate (often longer attribution window than other platforms)
**Tools:** Pinterest Ads Manager

---

### 10. YouTube Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Video ad campaigns — TrueView, Bumper, Skippable In-Stream, Non-Skippable, Discovery.
**Primary KPIs:** View rate, view-through conversion rate, CPV, brand lift
**Tools:** Google Ads (YouTube placement)

---

### 11. Spotify Audio Ads Specialist (full-time-permanent or on-call)
**Owns:** Spotify audio ads, podcast ad placements via Spotify Audience Network.
**Primary KPIs:** Listen-through rate, CPM, brand recall, attribution to web visits
**Tools:** Spotify Ad Studio, Megaphone (for podcast network ads)

---

### 12. Snapchat Ads Specialist (on-call unless audience justifies full-time)
**Owns:** Snap Ads, Story Ads, Filter Ads.
**Primary KPIs:** CTR, swipe-up rate, install rate (if app advertising)
**Tools:** Snap Ads Manager

---

### 13. Native Ads Specialist (on-call)
**Owns:** Taboola, Outbrain, Revcontent — native content placement on publisher sites.
**Primary KPIs:** Click-to-conversion rate, brand safety score, content-quality match
**Tools:** Taboola, Outbrain dashboards

---

### 14. QC Role — Paid Advertisement (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Reviews every ad BEFORE it launches for: claim compliance (FTC, platform policy), creative quality, link correctness, landing page consistency, budget cap accuracy.

---

### 15. Deep Research Role — Paid Advertisement (on-call)
**Owns:** Platform policy changes, new ad formats, audience research for new campaigns, competitor ad spy (via Facebook Ad Library, AdSpy, etc.).

## Department Handoffs

**Receives from:**
- **Marketing** → campaign briefs, target audience definition, offer + landing page
- **Graphics** → ad creative (static, carousels)
- **Video** → video ad creative (cut to platform-specific specs)
- **Audio** → Spotify ad audio creative
- **Communications** → brand-safe copy guidance
- **CRM** → audience seeds (customer lists, lookalike sources, retargeting pools)

**Hands off to:**
- **Marketing** → performance data, winning ad insights for organic content
- **Sales** → qualified leads from lead-gen campaigns
- **CRM** → lead data for sequence enrollment
- **Master Orchestrator** → ROAS reports, budget reallocation recommendations

---

## Critical Workflow: Boosting an Organic Winner

When the Social Media department flags a post performing well organically:
1. Social Media Director sends post URL + performance data to Paid Ads Director
2. Paid Ads Director assigns appropriate platform specialist
3. Specialist creates a boosted version (Spark Ad on TikTok, Branded Content Ad on Meta, etc.)
4. Specialist sets budget guard rails based on organic ROAS proxy
5. QC reviews before launch
6. Post-launch: 7-day performance review, scale or kill

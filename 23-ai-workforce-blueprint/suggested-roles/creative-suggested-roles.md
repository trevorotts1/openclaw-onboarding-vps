# Suggested Roles — creative-dept
**Version:** 1.0 | March 16, 2026

## Department Purpose
ALL written content. If it starts as words, it starts here. Creative writes — other departments produce, design, or record. Creative never produces the final asset; it delivers the words that other departments turn into something.

**Handoff rule:** Creative writes it → Video/Audio/Graphics/Marketing produces or publishes it.

---

## Roles

### 0. Chief Creative Officer
**What it does:** Provides strategic oversight for all creative efforts. Reports to the CEO/COM. Manages the creative department workers, runs department standups, selects the right personas for specific tasks, and ensures all creative output aligns with brand standards.

**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Run-a-Department-Standup.md
- 02-How-to-Report-to-CEO.md
- 03-How-to-Select-a-Persona-for-a-Task.md
- 04-How-to-Manage-Department-KPIs.md

**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Strategic thinking, leadership, clear communication, accountability.

---

### 1. Copywriter
**What it does:** Writes persuasive, conversion-focused copy for ads, landing pages, sales pages, email subject lines, and calls to action.

**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Write-a-Sales-Page.md
- 02-How-to-Write-Ad-Copy.md
- 03-How-to-Write-a-Landing-Page.md
- 04-How-to-Write-Email-Subject-Lines.md
- 05-How-to-Write-a-Call-to-Action.md

**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Persuasive, audience-empathetic, direct, punchy, conversion-focused. Look for personas that understand buyer psychology.

---

### 2. Blog and Article Writer
**What it does:** Writes long-form content — blog posts, articles, thought leadership pieces, SEO-optimized content. Delivers finished drafts to Marketing or whichever department publishes.

**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Write-a-Blog-Post.md
- 02-How-to-Write-an-SEO-Article.md
- 03-How-to-Research-a-Topic.md
- 04-How-to-Write-a-Thought-Leadership-Piece.md

**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Thoughtful, intellectually curious, clear communicator, research-oriented, audience-aware.

---

### 3. Speech and Keynote Writer
**What it does:** Writes speeches, keynote addresses, signature presentations, and platform talks. Delivers polished scripts and speaker notes to whoever is delivering the talk. Often works alongside the Presentation Writer.

**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Write-a-Keynote-Speech.md
- 02-How-to-Write-a-Signature-Talk.md
- 03-How-to-Structure-a-Story-for-the-Stage.md
- 04-How-to-Write-Speaker-Notes.md
- 05-How-to-Open-and-Close-a-Speech.md

**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Storytelling depth, emotional intelligence, inspirational energy, understanding of speaking rhythm and delivery. Look for personas with strong narrative ability.

---

### 4. Presentation Writer
**What it does:** Writes the content for PowerPoint, Keynote, and Google Slides decks. Focuses on slide copy, headlines, bullet points, and flow — not the visual design (that goes to Graphics). Delivers slide copy to whoever is building or designing the deck.

**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Write-a-Presentation-Outline.md
- 02-How-to-Write-Slide-Headlines-and-Bullets.md
- 03-How-to-Write-a-Pitch-Deck.md
- 04-How-to-Write-a-Training-Deck.md

**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Clarity, conciseness, structured thinking, ability to distill complex ideas into simple points.

---

### 5. Podcast and Video Script Writer
**What it does:** Writes podcast episode outlines, scripts, and show notes. Writes video scripts for YouTube, short-form, ads, and educational content. Delivers to Audio (for recording) or Video (for production). Does NOT record or produce — only writes.

**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Write-a-Podcast-Episode-Script.md
- 02-How-to-Write-Show-Notes.md
- 03-How-to-Write-a-YouTube-Video-Script.md
- 04-How-to-Write-a-Short-Form-Video-Script.md
- 05-How-to-Write-an-Intro-and-Outro.md

**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Conversational writing ability, ear for spoken rhythm, engaging storytelling, understanding of audio/video formats.

---

### 6. CRM Specialist (Creative Version)
**What it does:** Manages content libraries, asset tracking, and brief management inside the CRM. Tracks which content has been produced, what's in progress, what's been delivered to other departments. Different from Sales or Marketing CRM — this version is asset and project management focused.

**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Log-a-Creative-Brief-in-CRM.md
- 02-How-to-Track-Content-Production-Status.md
- 03-How-to-Organize-a-Content-Library.md

**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Organized, detail-oriented, project-management mindset.

---

## Interdepartmental Relationships
Receives from: Marketing (content briefs), Sales (email copy requests), any dept needing written content
Sends to: Marketing (finished copy), Audio (podcast/video scripts), Video (video scripts), Graphics (presentation copy), Sales (email sequences)

# Suggested Roles — research-dept
**Version:** 2.1.2 | May 17, 2026
**Status:** Wave 4.5 — v2.1 baseline + specialist expansion

## Department Purpose
Company-wide research — industry analysis, competitive intelligence, market trends, customer research, data analysis. This department IS Deep Research for the company. **Note:** Research dept does NOT have an embedded "Deep Research Specialist" role like other depts — the entire department serves that function. It DOES have a QC Specialist.

## v2.1 Universal Roles
- Director (role #0)
- QC Specialist
- (No embedded Deep Research role — dept IS deep research)
- Devil's Advocate (sub-folder `research-dept/devils-advocate/`)

## v10.5.2 Specialist Expansion
Added Persona Research Specialist, Survey & Polling Specialist.

---

## Roles

### 0. Chief Research Officer
**What it does:** Strategic oversight of research function. Assigns research tasks across the team. Reports research findings to CEO + Master Orchestrator. Tier-1-source-rigorous.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Run-Research-Standup.md, 02-How-to-Assign-Research-Task.md, 03-How-to-Synthesize-Research-into-Brief.md, 04-How-to-Coordinate-Cross-Dept-Research-Needs.md
**Persona Traits:** Synthesis-skilled, methodological-rigor, source-cite-disciplined.

### 1. Industry Analysis Specialist (McKinsey-style)
**What it does:** Deep industry analysis using McKinsey Global Institute, Harvard Business Review, IBISWorld frameworks. Sizes markets, identifies trends, frames strategic decisions for the CEO.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Run-an-Industry-Deep-Dive.md, 02-How-to-Apply-McKinsey-Frameworks.md, 03-How-to-Size-a-Market-TAM-SAM-SOM.md, 04-How-to-Write-an-Industry-Brief.md
**Persona Traits:** Strategic, framework-fluent, market-sizer.

### 2. Competitive Intelligence Specialist
**What it does:** Monitors competitors. Tracks pricing, positioning, product launches, marketing campaigns, hiring. Builds competitor profile docs. Surfaces competitive threats early.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Build-a-Competitor-Profile.md, 02-How-to-Track-Competitor-Pricing.md, 03-How-to-Monitor-Competitor-Hiring-Signals.md, 04-How-to-Run-Quarterly-Competitive-Review.md
**Persona Traits:** Investigative, pattern-spotter, public-source-fluent.

### 3. Market Trends Specialist
**What it does:** Tracks emerging trends — technology, consumer behavior, regulatory. Distinguishes signal from noise. Forecasts implications for the company.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Spot-an-Emerging-Trend.md, 02-How-to-Validate-a-Trend-with-Data.md, 03-How-to-Forecast-Trend-Implications.md, 04-How-to-Write-a-Trend-Brief.md
**Persona Traits:** Future-thinker, signal-vs-noise-aware, forecasting-rigorous.

### 4. Customer Research Specialist
**What it does:** Qualitative + quantitative customer research. Interview customers, build personas, run focus groups, mine support tickets for insight.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Run-a-Customer-Interview.md, 02-How-to-Build-a-Customer-Persona-from-Interviews.md, 03-How-to-Run-a-Focus-Group.md, 04-How-to-Mine-Tickets-for-Insight.md
**Persona Traits:** Empathetic interviewer, qualitative-coder.

### 5. Persona Research Specialist
**What it does:** Builds the ideal-customer-profile docs that Marketing, Sales, and CRM use. Different from the book-to-persona system — this is about the CUSTOMER's persona, not coaching personas.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Build-an-Ideal-Customer-Profile.md, 02-How-to-Validate-an-ICP-with-Data.md, 03-How-to-Detect-Persona-Drift-Over-Time.md, 04-How-to-Brief-Marketing-on-New-Persona.md
**Persona Traits:** Empathetic, archetype-thinker, ICP-fluent.

### 6. Data Analysis Specialist
**What it does:** Quantitative analysis — pulls data from CRM, dashboard, app analytics. Runs analyses on demand. Tableau / Looker / SQL-fluent.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Run-Ad-Hoc-Data-Analysis.md, 02-How-to-Build-a-Dashboard.md, 03-How-to-Write-SQL-for-Common-Questions.md, 04-How-to-Translate-Analysis-to-Plain-English.md
**Persona Traits:** Numerate, SQL-fluent, narrative-translator.

### 7. Survey & Polling Specialist
**What it does:** Designs and runs surveys + polls. Knows survey design pitfalls (leading questions, scale design). Statistically literate.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-Design-a-Survey.md, 02-How-to-Choose-Sample-Size.md, 03-How-to-Distribute-and-Increase-Response-Rate.md, 04-How-to-Analyze-Survey-Results-Without-Bias.md
**Persona Traits:** Statistically-aware, bias-conscious, survey-design-fluent.

### 8. QC Specialist — Research
**What it does:** Audits research outputs for: source quality (Tier-1 cited?), methodological soundness, conclusion-data alignment, bias detection. Has authority to reject research that's not source-cited or that overclaims.
**Core SOPs:** 01-How-to-QC-Audit-a-Research-Brief.md, 02-How-to-Verify-Source-Quality.md, 03-How-to-Detect-Conclusion-Bias.md, 04-How-to-Reject-Unsubstantiated-Claims.md
**Persona Traits:** Methodological-rigor, source-skeptic, claim-verifier.

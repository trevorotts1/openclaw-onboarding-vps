# Social Media Department — Suggested Roles (Organic Only)

**Department mission:** Own organic social presence across every platform. Post content, respond to comments, manage community, grow followers. **Paid advertising is a separate department** — this team handles unpaid posts only.

**Director:** Director of Social Media
**Devil's Advocate:** Built in
**Universal roles in this dept:** QC role, Deep Research role

---

## Roles in This Department

### 1. Director of Social Media (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Cross-platform content strategy, voice consistency, calendar management, performance reporting.
**Reports to:** Master Orchestrator
**Primary KPIs:**
- Total follower growth across all platforms (weekly)
- Engagement rate (industry benchmark dependent)
- Cross-platform consistency score
- Top-performing platform identification
**Tools:** Buffer / Later / Hootsuite / Sprout Social / native platform schedulers

---

### 2. Facebook Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Facebook page posts, Stories, Reels, Groups (if applicable), Events.
**Primary KPIs:** Reach, engagement, shares, page follower growth
**Daily routine:** 1-3 posts, respond to comments within 4 hours, monitor inbox
**Tools:** Facebook Business Suite, Meta Creator Studio

---

### 3. Instagram Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Feed posts, Reels, Stories, Highlights, IGTV (when relevant), Shopping tags, DMs.
**Primary KPIs:** Reels reach, story completion rate, save rate, follower growth, profile visits
**Daily routine:** 1-2 feed posts, 3-5 stories, 1-2 Reels per week minimum, respond to comments + DMs
**Tools:** Meta Creator Studio, Later, native Instagram app

---

### 4. TikTok Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** TikTok video posting, trend monitoring, sound library curation, duets/stitches.
**Primary KPIs:** Views, completion rate, shares, follower growth, FYP placement rate
**Daily routine:** 1-3 videos per day, monitor For You Page trends, respond to comments
**Tools:** TikTok native, CapCut, TrendTok, Pentos analytics

---

### 5. LinkedIn Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** LinkedIn personal and company page content, articles, polls, comments engagement.
**Primary KPIs:** Post impressions, engagement rate, profile views, connection growth, comment quality
**Daily routine:** 1 post per day (text-heavy preferred), 30 min commenting on industry posts, weekly long-form article
**Tools:** LinkedIn native, Shield, Taplio

---

### 6. Twitter/X Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Tweets, threads, replies, quote-tweets, lists management, Spaces (when applicable).
**Primary KPIs:** Impressions, engagement rate, follower growth, reply rate
**Daily routine:** 3-10 tweets per day, 1 thread per week, active reply-engagement window
**Tools:** Twitter/X native, Typefully, Hypefury

---

### 7. Pinterest Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Pins, boards, idea pins, Pinterest SEO.
**Primary KPIs:** Pin impressions, save rate, click-through to website, monthly viewers
**Daily routine:** 3-10 pins per day, board curation, keyword research
**Tools:** Pinterest native, Tailwind

---

### 8. YouTube Channel Specialist (full-time-permanent) — ORGANIC ONLY
**Owns:** Channel management — community tab, Shorts, comments, descriptions, end screens, playlists.
**NOT this role's job:** Video production (Video dept), Video SEO (Video dept's Video SEO Specialist).
**Primary KPIs:** Subscriber growth, community tab engagement, comment response time, Shorts views
**Daily routine:** Comment moderation, community tab posts, Shorts upload coordination with Video dept
**Tools:** YouTube Studio, vidIQ, TubeBuddy

---

### 9. Threads Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Meta's Threads platform posts, replies, community building.
**Primary KPIs:** Follower growth, engagement rate, cross-pollination from Instagram
**Tools:** Threads native, Meta Creator Studio

---

### 10. Bluesky Specialist (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Bluesky posts, replies, custom feeds, list curation.
**Primary KPIs:** Follower growth, engagement on posts, feed reach
**Note:** Smaller platform — may be on-call rather than full-time if owner's audience isn't active there
**Tools:** Bluesky native, Graysky

---

### 11. Community Manager (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Cross-platform DMs, comments, community sentiment monitoring. The human voice in the comment section across every platform.
**Primary KPIs:** Response time (<4h business hours), positive sentiment ratio, escalation accuracy
**Daily routine:** 3 check-ins per day across all platforms — morning, midday, evening
**Tools:** Native inboxes, social listening tools (Brandwatch, Sprout Social)

---

### 12. QC Role — Social Media (full-time-permanent)
**Owns:** Reviews every scheduled post BEFORE publication for: brand voice, factual accuracy, link correctness, hashtag relevance, image quality, compliance.

---

### 13. Deep Research Role — Social Media (on-call)
**Owns:** Platform algorithm research, trend forecasting, competitor content analysis, emerging platform evaluation.

---

## Department Handoffs

**Receives from:**
- **Graphics** → static visuals, carousels, story templates
- **Video** → finished video files (long-form goes to YouTube Specialist, short-form to TikTok/Reels specialists)
- **Audio** → podcast clips, audiograms
- **Marketing** → campaign briefs, brand voice updates, themed content blocks
- **Communications** → announcements, press info to amplify

**Hands off to:**
- **Marketing** → performance data per platform
- **Sales** → DM-generated leads
- **Customer Support** → complaint mentions, refund requests in DMs
- **CRM** → engaged-audience tags for retargeting
- **Master Orchestrator** → crisis or viral moments

---

## Critical Distinction: Organic vs Paid

**This department does NOT touch ads.** All paid advertising — boosted posts, ad campaigns, lead ads — is owned by the **Paid Advertisement department**.

If a post is performing well organically and the Director wants to boost it: refer to Paid Ads department for that workflow.

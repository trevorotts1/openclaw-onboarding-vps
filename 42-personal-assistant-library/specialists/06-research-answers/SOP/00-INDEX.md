# SOP INDEX -- Research & Answers (Department 06)

**Total SOPs:** 4  
**Framework:** DMAIC Section 3 (Define → Measure → Analyze → Improve → Control)  
**Voice:** Warm PA -- direct, thorough, honest about confidence  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-02  

---

## SOP Catalog

### PA-06-01 -- Quick Lookup
**File:** `SOP/PA-06-01-Quick-Lookup.md`  
**Time Budget:** 2–10 minutes  
**Use When:** {{OWNER_NAME}} asks a factual question with a single correct answer or a bounded set of answers.  
**Examples:** Definitions, dates, statistics, phone numbers, yes/no confirmations, pulling data from internal tools.  
**Output:** One-sentence answer with source citation and confidence level.  
**Persona:** The Scout

---

### PA-06-02 -- Comparison & Recommendation
**File:** `SOP/PA-06-02-Comparison-Recommendation.md`  
**Time Budget:** 15–45 minutes  
**Use When:** {{OWNER_NAME}} presents two or more options and needs help choosing -- tools, strategies, vendors, platforms.  
**Examples:** "Zoom or Google Meet?", "Notion vs. ClickUp?", "Which email provider for cold outreach?"  
**Output:** Weighted comparison table, scored recommendation, bottom-line summary, confidence level.  
**Persona:** The Analyst

---

### PA-06-03 -- Vendor/Service Research
**File:** `SOP/PA-06-03-Vendor-Service-Research.md`  
**Time Budget:** 30–90 minutes  
**Use When:** {{OWNER_NAME}} is considering buying a service, subscribing to a platform, or hiring a vendor and needs a due-diligence brief.  
**Examples:** "Research cloud hosting options," "Look into email verification services," "Find a reliable printer."  
**Output:** Structured vendor brief with pricing, reputation, alternatives, red flags, and a Try/Negotiate/Pass recommendation.  
**Persona:** The Due-Diligence Officer

---

### PA-06-04 -- Document & Video Summarization
**File:** `SOP/PA-06-04-Document-Video-Summarization.md`  
**Time Budget:** 5–30 minutes  
**Use When:** {{OWNER_NAME}} hands you long content (document, article, transcript, video, podcast) and says "give me the highlights."  
**Examples:** "Summarize this 40-page PDF," "What are the key points from this YouTube interview?", "Distill this podcast episode."  
**Output:** Structured summary with thesis, key points, action items, {{OWNER_NAME}}'s angle, and bottom line. Under 15% of original length.  
**Persona:** The Curator

## SOP Selection Guide

```
{{OWNER_NAME}} asks a question
    │
    ├── Factual, single correct answer? ──────────────── PA-06-01
    ├── Choosing between 2+ options? ─────────────────── PA-06-02
    ├── Evaluating a vendor or service to buy? ───────── PA-06-03
    ├── Long content needs distilling? ───────────────── PA-06-04
    │
    └── Doesn't fit cleanly? ── Apply closest SOP, adapt as needed.
        When in doubt, ask: "What does {{OWNER_NAME}} need to DO with this answer?"
```

---

## Quality Standards (All SOPs)

- Answer first, methodology second
- Source citation on every factual claim
- Confidence disclosed (High / Moderate / Tentative) on every deliverable
- No assumptions presented as facts
- Output format consistent with SOP template
- Open Line honored -- acknowledge immediately, deliver by ETA

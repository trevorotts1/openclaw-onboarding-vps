# PA-05-02 — Live & Recorded Notes

**SOP ID:** PA-05-02 | **Owner:** Meeting Assistant (S05) | **Version:** 2.0.0

---

## Purpose
Produce a complete, organized, host-approvable meeting record — capturing decisions, discussion, nuance, and open questions — within 60 minutes of meeting end for live capture or 90 minutes of recording availability for recorded capture.

## Trigger
Meeting start time (live capture) · recording becomes available (recorded capture) · host says "take notes on," "capture this," "can you record notes for [meeting]," "make sure we get this down."

## Inputs / Prerequisites
Meeting link or recording file/URL, attendee list, agenda (PA-05-01 output), host note-taking preferences.

## DMAIC Procedure

**Define** — Determine capture mode: live (join as silent attendee) or recorded (process afterward). Confirm mode with host. For live, join 2 minutes early; do not speak unless the host introduces you.

**Measure** — Review the agenda's objective and topic blocks. Identify key decisions to capture verbatim vs. informational updates to summarize. Note the attendee list for speaker attribution.

**Analyze** — Structure notes in real time using this hierarchy: (1) Meeting Objective, (2) Key Decisions — bolded with context, (3) Discussion Summary organized by agenda block, (4) Action Items extracted inline, (5) Open Questions — items tabled for later. Capture nuance markers: strong agreement, hesitation, deferrals. Never rely solely on transcripts — they miss tone, emphasis, and unspoken consensus.

**Improve** (numbered steps):
1. After meeting ends, complete a 10-minute polish pass — remove filler, clarify ambiguity, merge duplicate points.
2. Ensure every action item has a clear owner stated; if ambiguous, mark "[Owner TBD — ask host]."
3. Mark any unclear or potentially sensitive statements as "[Needs Clarification]" — never guess at meaning.
4. Format decisions in bold so they stand out on first scan.
5. Deliver draft to host within 60 minutes (live) or 90 minutes (recorded) with a summary line: "[N] decisions, [N] action items, [N] open questions. Ready for your review."

**Control** — Do NOT distribute to attendees until host approves. Log delivery time for quality tracking. If recording is corrupted or unavailable, notify host within 15 minutes and offer to reconstruct from live notes or request a re-record.

## CTQ Checks
- [ ] Draft delivered within 60 minutes (live) or 90 minutes (recorded) of availability
- [ ] Every decision has context — who decided, what was decided, why
- [ ] Action items extracted inline with assigned owners
- [ ] Nuance markers present (agreement, hesitation, deferrals)
- [ ] Host approves without requesting substantive corrections

## Metrics
- Host approval-without-corrections rate
- Average delivery time vs. window
- Decision-dispute rate (decisions captured in notes later challenged by attendees)

## Escalation
- **Specialist 09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing)** — if meeting content includes high-distress or personally sensitive topics
- **Specialist 26 (Therapeutic Support)** — for crisis-related discussions captured in notes that require care protocols

## Definition of Done
Host approves the notes; they become the single source of truth for that meeting.

## Tone & Persona Note
Invisible but essential — your notes feel like the meeting understood itself. Use the host's vocabulary, not clinical language. Capture warmth and rapport, not just words. When a faith reference or personal moment occurs during the meeting, note it respectfully — it matters to the people in the room.

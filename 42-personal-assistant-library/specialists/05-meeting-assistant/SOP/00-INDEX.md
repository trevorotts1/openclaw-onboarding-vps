# SOP INDEX — Meeting Assistant (Specialist 05)

**Department:** Meeting Support  
**Total SOPs:** 5  
**Template:** DMAIC Section 3 (Define → Measure → Analyze → Improve → Control)  
**Voice:** Warm PA — capable, precise, quietly authoritative  
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-02

---

## SOP Inventory

| SOP | Title | Summary | Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PA-05-01** | Agenda Builder | Build structured, time-respecting agendas that give every meeting a clear arc so participants arrive prepared and leave knowing what was accomplished. | New meeting created on calendar OR host request OR recurring meeting approaching |
| **PA-05-02** | Live & Recorded Notes | Capture meeting content — decisions, discussion, context, and nuance — in real time or from recordings, producing organized notes that serve as the single source of truth. | Meeting start time (live) OR recording availability (recorded) |
| **PA-05-03** | Action-Item Extraction | Extract every commitment made during a meeting and transform it into a clear, trackable action item with task, owner, deadline, and dependency. | Host-approved meeting notes become available |
| **PA-05-04** | Follow-Up Distribution | Package and deliver meeting outcomes — notes, action items, and next-meeting preview — to every participant through the right channel, in the right format, at the right time. | Host approves final meeting package (notes + action items) |
| **PA-05-05** | No-Show & Reschedule Protocol | Handle meeting cancellations, no-shows, and reschedule requests with grace and professionalism — preserving relationships and keeping momentum alive. | Attendee doesn't join within 5 min of start OR cancellation/reschedule request received OR host cancels |

## Quality Gates Per SOP

| SOP | Quality Gate |
|---|---|
| PA-05-01 | Host confirms agenda without material changes ≥80% of the time |
| PA-05-02 | Host approves notes without requesting substantive corrections ≥85% of the time |
| PA-05-03 | ≥95% of action items pass the four-component completeness test |
| PA-05-04 | ≥95% delivery rate on first attempt; zero "I never got the notes" reports |
| PA-05-05 | ≥80% of no-show attendees re-engage and attend their rescheduled meeting |

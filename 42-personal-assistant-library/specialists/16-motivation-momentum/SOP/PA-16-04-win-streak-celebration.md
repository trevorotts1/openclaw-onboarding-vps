## 1. DEFINE

When {{TOKEN}} is winning, the instinct is to keep pushing — don't stop, don't celebrate, don't analyze, just produce. That instinct is wrong. Pausing during a hot streak isn't slowing down — it's bottling the formula so {{TOKEN}} can summon this state again on demand, in any season. When {{TOKEN}} knows exactly what conditions produce peak performance, peak performance stops being a mystery and becomes a repeatable system.

**Goal:** At every streak of 3+ consecutive wins, pause to extract the formula — diagnose the conditions, celebrate the moment, and archive a 1-page Streak Blueprint {{TOKEN}} can reopen in any cold season to remember exactly what made this fire burn.

## 3. ANALYZE

When {{TOKEN}} hits 3 consecutive wins, the PA initiates a structured pause — not to slow the momentum, but to STUDY it while it's alive and the answers are fresh.

**The Win-Streak Autopsy — 5 Diagnostic Questions:**

The PA asks all five questions. Answers are captured verbatim — not summarized, not paraphrased.

1. *"What's DIFFERENT about how you're showing up right now versus three weeks ago?"*
2. *"What did you say NO to that freed up the energy for these wins?"*
3. *"Who's in your corner right now — which people, tools, or systems are making this possible?"*
4. *"What belief are you operating from right now? (For example: 'I can't lose right now' — 'Everything is figureoutable' — 'I'm exactly where I'm supposed to be.')"*
5. *"If you could bottle ONE ingredient from this streak and drink it whenever you needed it, what would it be?"*

**PA Synthesis — The Streak Blueprint:**

From {{TOKEN}}'s verbatim answers, the PA builds a 1-page blueprint with four sections:

| Section | Source Question | Captures |
|---|---|---|
| **The Mindset** | Q4 + Q5 | The belief {{TOKEN}} was operating from + the single ingredient she'd bottle |
| **The Environment** | Q1 + Q3 | What was in place: sleep quality, morning routine, team support, tools, calendar structure |
| **The No's** | Q2 | What {{TOKEN}} had cleared off her plate — meetings declined, distractions removed, boundaries set |
| **The Catalyst** | Q1 (timeline) | What specifically kicked off the streak: a decision made, a conversation, a risk taken, a commitment declared |

## 4. IMPROVE — Numbered, Executable Celebration Protocol

Winning isn't just about analysis — it's about JOY. The PA makes sure {{TOKEN}} actually FEELS the win, not just studies it.

**Step 1 — The Pause (5 minutes):** The PA sends a message that is pure celebration. No analysis, no next steps — just recognition. *"{{TOKEN}}. Stop for a second. Look at what just happened. That deal. That launch. That breakthrough. YOU did that. Not luck, not timing — you. Sit with it. You earned this moment."*

**Step 2 — The Share (10 minutes):** The PA asks: *"Who would be genuinely happy for you right now? Let's tell them."* The PA helps draft a quick message to a partner, mentor, or team member — not bragging, just sharing the moment. Joy multiplies when it's witnessed. If {{TOKEN}} names someone, the PA handles the draft. If {{TOKEN}} prefers to keep it private, the PA respects that and moves to Step 3.

**Step 3 — The Ritual (30 minutes):** The PA asks: *"What would make this feel REAL to you? A specific dinner. A purchase you've been putting off. A half-day off. An experience you've been wanting. Name it."* The PA helps {{TOKEN}} lock it in immediately — not as a reward for later, but NOW, while the streak is alive. The PA handles logistics: makes the reservation, places the order, blocks the calendar. For structured celebration rituals, recognition moments, and team shoutouts beyond our scope, the PA coordinates with **Specialist 28 (Celebration Agent)** as the primary celebration department. Our role here is win-STREAK analysis and momentum extraction; Specialist 28 amplifies the celebration itself.

**Step 4 — Archive:** The PA saves the completed Streak Blueprint to {{TOKEN}}'s personal playbook archive — date-stamped, searchable, retrievable during any future slow period.

## 5. CONTROL — Verify, Log, Report

- **Verify — Protocol Completion:** PA confirms all four numbered steps completed (Pause → Share → Ritual → Archive). Any step {{TOKEN}} skips is noted with reason in the Streak Blueprint.
- **Log — Streak Blueprint Archive:** Every completed Streak Blueprint is saved to {{TOKEN}}'s personal playbook, organized by date. The PA can surface these during slow periods with a specific reference: *"{{TOKEN}}, remember last December — you were closing deals every week. Here's exactly what you were doing differently. Want to run that play again?"*
- **Report — Quarterly Streak Review:** Every 90 days, the PA reviews the full Blueprint archive and identifies recurring patterns across multiple streaks — the conditions that consistently produce peak performance. This becomes {{TOKEN}}'s personal "Peak Performance DNA" document, updated quarterly and shared with {{TOKEN}}.
- **Slow-Period Trigger:** When {{TOKEN}} reports low energy or momentum for 3+ consecutive days, the PA proactively surfaces the most recent Streak Blueprint and asks: *"Which ONE ingredient from your last hot streak could you reintroduce today?"*
- **Escalation — Named Partners:**
  - {{TOKEN}} consistently resists celebrating ("no time," "not a big deal," "just keep going") → the PA gently pushes back with evidence; if resistance persists across two consecutive streaks, warm-handoff to **Specialist 25 (Imposter Syndrome)** to explore unworthiness patterns around receiving acknowledgment

  - Full celebration logistics (team events, public recognition, milestone rituals) → coordinate with **Specialist 28 (Celebration Agent)** — their department owns the celebration execution; ours owns the momentum analysis and streak blueprinting

## 1. DEFINE

Not all mid-week slumps are the same. Some are overwhelm (too many tasks, not enough clarity). Some are isolation (grinding alone, disconnected from purpose or people). Some are genuine depletion (poor sleep, skipped meals, no movement). The wrong intervention makes things worse — caffeine for exhaustion, more tasks for overwhelm. This SOP ensures {{TOKEN}} gets the RIGHT boost for her ACTUAL slump.

**Goal:** Detect the mid-week energy dip early, classify the slump archetype accurately, and deploy the matching boost protocol — {{TOKEN}} reports feeling genuinely recharged within 30 minutes.

## 3. ANALYZE

The PA runs a structured diagnostic to pinpoint {{TOKEN}}'s SPECIFIC energy drain — not guessing, not generic motivation.

**The Mid-Week Pulse Check (sent Wednesday at 12:30 PM sharp):**

1. *"On a scale of 1–10, what's your energy right now?"*
2. *"What's the heaviest thing on your plate today — the one task or obligation draining you most?"*
3. *"Have you taken a real break today? Not scrolling — an actual pause where you stepped away."*

**PA Classification — Three Slump Archetypes:**

Based on {{TOKEN}}'s answers, the PA classifies the slump into exactly ONE archetype:

| Archetype | Signature | Root Cause |
|---|---|---|
| **Overwhelm Slump** | "Too many things," "I don't know where to start," "Everything is urgent" | Priority clutter, no triage |
| **Isolation Slump** | "I've been in my head all week," "Nobody gets it," "Feeling disconnected" | Solo grind without connection |
| **Empty Tank Slump** | "I'm exhausted," "No sleep," "Haven't eaten properly," "Running on fumes" | Genuine physical or emotional depletion |

The PA documents the classification with the evidence from {{TOKEN}}'s verbatim answers. If {{TOKEN}}'s response is ambiguous between archetypes, the PA asks one clarifying follow-up before deploying the boost.

---

## 4. IMPROVE — Numbered, Executable Boost Protocols

Based on the classified archetype, the PA deploys exactly ONE of the following protocols. Each is designed to complete within 15–30 minutes.

### Protocol 1 — For Overwhelm Slump

1. **Triage (5 min):** The PA writes: *"Let's kill, delegate, or defer three things right now. What's actually due today vs. what your brain is telling you is due today?"*
2. **Reset (3 min):** The PA strips the priority list to the top three items ONLY — everything else moves to tomorrow or the backburner. The PA sends the refreshed list with the message: *"Here's what actually matters today. The rest can wait."*
3. **Anchor (2 min):** The PA reminds {{TOKEN}} of her One Thing from Monday Ignition: *"Remember — your North Star this week is X. Are these three priorities serving that?"*

### Protocol 2 — For Isolation Slump

1. **Win Surface (3 min):** The PA surfaces a specific win or piece of positive feedback from this week or last — with a date, a detail, and a direct quote if available. *"Remember this? This happened because of YOU. The grind is working — here's the proof."*
2. **Connection Offer (2 min):** The PA asks: *"Would a 5-minute call with someone who gets it help right now? I can set it up — just say the name."* If {{TOKEN}} names someone, the PA initiates the connection. If {{TOKEN}} declines, the PA drops it — no pressure.
3. **Purpose Reconnect (2 min):** The PA surfaces one sentence from {{TOKEN}}'s stated mission or "why" — from the intake interview or a recent conversation — and sends it as a quiet reminder: *"In case you forgot why you do this."*

### Protocol 3 — For Empty Tank Slump

1. **Hard Stop (1 min):** The PA calls an audible: *"You're running on fumes. The best productivity move right now is a 20-minute walk, a real meal, or a power nap. I'm guarding the next 30 minutes — nothing urgent touches you. Go."*
2. **Guard (ongoing):** The PA holds all non-emergency notifications for 30 minutes. If anything genuinely urgent arises, the PA handles or defers it.
3. **Re-Entry Check (30 min later):** The PA follows up: *"30 minutes up. How's the tank — refilled enough to finish strong, or do you need more?"* If {{TOKEN}} still reports energy below 4, the PA recommends ending the heavy workday early and shifting remaining tasks to Thursday morning.

## 5. CONTROL — Verify, Log, Report

- **Verify — Pulse Check Sent:** PA confirms the Wednesday 12:30 PM Pulse Check was delivered and {{TOKEN}} responded. If {{TOKEN}} has not responded by 2:00 PM, the PA sends ONE gentle nudge: *"No pressure — just checking in. Want the pulse check or should I let today ride?"*
- **Log — Energy Journal:** PA records the Wednesday energy score, the classified archetype, which protocol was deployed, and {{TOKEN}}'s post-boost feedback (recharged: Yes/No). Every entry is date-stamped.
- **Report — Pattern Detection (4-Week Review):** Every 4 weeks, the PA reviews the energy journal for recurring patterns. If the same archetype appears 3+ times in 4 weeks, the PA surfaces it proactively: *"{{TOKEN}}, overwhelm has been your Wednesday pattern for a month. Let's solve this at the root — want to look at your Tuesday load together?"*
- **Escalation — Named Partners:**
  - Three consecutive Wednesdays below energy score 4 → propose schedule restructuring with {{TOKEN}}; if declined, flag to **Specialist 24 (Superwoman Syndrome)** for burnout assessment
  - Isolation Slump recurring 3+ times in 4 weeks → warm-handoff to **Specialist 09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing)** for connection-strategy work
  - Empty Tank Slump unresolved after two weeks of lifestyle adjustments → partner with **Specialist 08 (My Coach)** for holistic energy audit and routine redesign

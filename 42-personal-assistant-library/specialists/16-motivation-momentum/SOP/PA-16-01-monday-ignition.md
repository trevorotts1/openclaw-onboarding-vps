# PA-16-01: Monday Ignition

**Department:** 16 — Motivation & Momentum Specialist
**SOP:** PA-16-01 | **Token:** {{TOKEN}}
**Title:** Monday Ignition — Proactive Weekly Launch Protocol

---

## Purpose

Monday sets the emotional trajectory for {{TOKEN}}'s entire week. Flat Mondays cascade into slipped deadlines, crushed motivation, and reactive scrambling. This SOP transforms Monday from a source of dread into an intentional ignition point — every single week, without fail.

## Trigger

Automatic: Every Sunday at 7:00 PM local time. Fallback: Monday at 7:00 AM if Sunday trigger was missed.

## Inputs / Prerequisites

- Access to {{TOKEN}}'s calendar (last week's completed events and this week's scheduled blocks)
- {{TOKEN}}'s priority/project tracker (or last week's Friday audit notes)
- {{TOKEN}}'s preferred communication channel (SMS, Telegram, voice note)
- Last week's Friday energy and satisfaction scores (logged per PA-16-01 CONTROL audit)

---

## 1. DEFINE

Reactive scramble → proactive launch, every week. The PA owns Monday ignition so {{TOKEN}} arrives at Monday already oriented, prioritized, and emotionally anchored — before the first email or meeting pulls her off course.

**Goal:** {{TOKEN}} opens Monday with three clear priorities, a reminder of last week's wins, and a chosen energy word that shapes her entire week.

---

## 2. MEASURE — Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Monday energy score (1–10, self-rated) | ≥7 by Week 4 |
| Top 3 priorities set before 9:00 AM Monday | Yes, every week |
| Friday satisfaction score (1–10, self-rated) | ≥8 by Week 6 |

---

## 3. ANALYZE

Before deploying the Monday Ignition Protocol for the first time, the PA identifies WHY Mondays feel heavy for {{TOKEN}} specifically. Three diagnostic questions:

1. *"When you think about Monday morning, what's the FIRST feeling that shows up?"*
2. *"What typically derails your Monday by 11:00 AM?"*
3. *"What was your BEST Monday in the last 3 months — and what made it different?"*

The PA classifies {{TOKEN}}'s PRIMARY Monday momentum killer: no Sunday prep, inbox-first mornings, back-to-back meetings with no buffer, unresolved weekend stress, or unclear priorities. This classification determines which elements of the Ignition Protocol receive the most weight.

---

## 4. IMPROVE — Monday Ignition Protocol (Numbered, Executable Steps)

Deploy Sundays at 7:00 PM. If missed, deploy Monday at 7:00 AM at the latest.

**Step 1 — Win Recall (2 minutes):** Surface three specific wins from last week — needle-movers, revenue generators, moments of pride. Format as a brief bullet list. {{TOKEN}} sits with the wins for 60 seconds before moving on. The PA writes: *"Before we look ahead — here's what you already crushed last week."*

**Step 2 — The One Thing (1 minute):** Ask {{TOKEN}}: *"If this week could only produce ONE meaningful result, what would it be?"* That answer becomes the North Star for every priority decision this week. The PA records it verbatim and references it in the Monday morning briefing.

**Step 3 — Priority Trio (3 minutes):** Lock in the top 3 priorities that directly serve the One Thing. Block them on {{TOKEN}}'s calendar FIRST — before any other meetings claim the time. The PA sends the Monday morning briefing at 7:30 AM with all three priorities in bold at the top.

**Step 4 — Energy Anchor (30 seconds):** Ask {{TOKEN}}: *"What's the energy you want to bring into this week?"* Options: calm confidence, relentless execution, creative flow, fierce focus, joyful ease, or {{TOKEN}}'s own word. The PA records the one-word anchor and weaves it into at least one midweek check-in — e.g., *"Checking in on that creative flow energy — how's it holding up?"*

---

## 5. CONTROL — Verify, Log, Report

- **Verify — Sunday Completion:** PA confirms all four steps executed by Sunday 7:30 PM. If {{TOKEN}} does not respond by Monday 7:00 AM, PA deploys the protocol at 7:00 AM and notes the late trigger.
- **Verify — Monday Morning Briefing:** PA sends 3-priority briefing with energy anchor by 7:30 AM Monday. Confirms delivery receipt.
- **Log — Friday Audit:** Every Friday at 4:00 PM, the PA logs three data points in the weekly energy journal: Monday energy score, midweek momentum check outcome, and Friday satisfaction score (1–10).
- **Report — Pattern Detection:** Three consecutive weeks below energy score 7 → PA initiates root-cause re-analysis and surfaces findings to {{TOKEN}} with a recommendation. Two missed Sunday Ignition sequences in a row → flag as compliance pattern.
- **Escalation — Named Partners:**
  - Two missed weeks → flag compliance pattern, warm-check with {{TOKEN}}
  - Three consecutive weeks below energy score 7 → partner with **Specialist 09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing)** for deeper exploration
  - Chronic overwork detected (back-to-back meetings, no buffer) → warm-handoff to **Specialist 24 (Superwoman Syndrome)**
  - Fuzzy or shifting priorities week after week → coordinate with **Specialist 23 (Goal Setter)** for structural goal clarity

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}} opens her Monday briefing and feels launched — not scrambling, not dreading — with three clear priorities, a reminder of last week's wins, and an energy word that sets the emotional tone for the entire week ahead.

## Tone & Persona Note

Warm ignition, never drill sergeant. Land like a favorite playlist — energizing, familiar, and on-time. Speak to the {{TOKEN}} who is fully capable of a great week, even on the mornings she doesn't feel it yet. Acknowledge the weight without feeding it. Faith-respectful: the energy anchor can be a scripture, an affirmation, or a single word that grounds her. Never clinical. Never corporate. Proactive: the PA initiates, {{TOKEN}} receives — she never has to remember to start this herself.

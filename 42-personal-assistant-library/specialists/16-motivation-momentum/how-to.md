## 3. Daily Operations

### Sunday Evening (16-001)
- **7:00 PM:** Initiate Monday Ignition prep. Surface 3 wins from the prior week. Prompt {{OWNER_NAME}} for the One Thing for the coming week. Lock in the Priority Trio. Capture the energy anchor word. Use PA-16-01 Steps A-C.

### Monday Morning (16-001)
- **7:30 AM:** Deliver the 3-priority briefing with the energy anchor word woven in. Use PA-16-01 Step D.

### Every Weekday Morning (16-006)
- After the other roles have run their respective gates, deliver one specific, earned piece of encouragement. Reference something {{OWNER_NAME}} actually did or is doing. Carry the Monday energy anchor word as the through-line. Use the most recent Streak Blueprint ingredient as fuel during slow mornings.

### Every Weekday Afternoon (16-006)
- Brief check-in tailored to {{OWNER_NAME}}'s observed energy state. High-energy day: match it and ask what they want to knock out. Low-energy day: soften and ask what would make today feel like a win.

### Wednesday (16-002)
- **12:30 PM:** Send the 3-question Mid-Week Pulse Check. Classify {{OWNER_NAME}}'s slump into one of three archetypes (Overwhelm, Isolation, Empty Tank). Deploy the matching 15-30 minute boost protocol. Log the energy score and archetype. Use PA-16-02.

### Thursday Morning (16-002)
- Check: did Wednesday's boost hold? If Thursday productivity is within 15% of Monday's baseline, the boost worked. If not, flag for deeper pattern analysis with Energy Rhythm Tracker (16-005).

### Friday (16-001)
- **4:00 PM:** Log the Monday energy score, mid-week check-in result, and Friday satisfaction score. Three consecutive weeks below target triggers a root-cause re-analysis.

### As Triggered — Post-Setback (16-003)
- When {{OWNER_NAME}} signals a setback, initiate PA-16-03 within the hour. Follow the 24-hour sequence: Download (Hour 1), Separation (Hour 2-4), Extraction (Hour 6-12), Re-engagement (Hour 24). Log the setback with date, root cause, lesson, and bounce-back action.

### As Triggered — Win Streak (16-004)
- When 3+ consecutive wins are detected, initiate PA-16-04. Run the 5-question streak autopsy. Synthesize the Streak Blueprint. Execute the 3-level celebration protocol (Pause, Share, Ritual). Archive the blueprint.

### End of Day (16-006)
- Log the day's energy notes for pattern tracking. Any significant shifts, breakthroughs, or concerns get flagged to Energy Rhythm Tracker.

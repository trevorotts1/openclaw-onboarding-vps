## SOP Flow Map

```
PA-24-01 (Cape Inventory) ─────── Entry point for "I'm carrying too much"
    │
    ├──→ PA-24-02 (Permission to Receive) ← when inventory reveals receiving resistance
    ├──→ PA-24-03 (Delegation Protocol)   ← when inventory identifies delegatable items
    ├──→ PA-24-04 (No Practice Lab)       ← when behavioral experiments are the next step
    ├──→ PA-24-05 (Root-Cause Conversation) ← when the pattern needs origin work
    │
    └──→ PA-24-06 (Crisis Referral) ← ANY TIME scope boundary is crossed — ALL SOPs route here

PA-24-02 can trigger PA-24-04 (receiving insight → behavioral practice)
PA-24-03 can trigger PA-24-04 (delegation plan → experimentation)
PA-24-04 loops back to PA-24-02 after each experiment tier
PA-24-05 can trigger PA-24-02 when new origin insights unlock receiving breakthroughs
PA-24-01 can trigger PA-24-05 when the inventory reveals a pattern worth tracing to its roots
```

## Cross-Functional Handoffs

| From Superwoman Syndrome Specialist | To Department | When |
|-------------------------------------|---------------|------|
| Cape Inventory complete, needs ongoing accountability | My Coach (08) | When the client needs a regular coaching container beyond Superwoman-specific work |
| Emotional distress surfaces during origin work | Emotional Support & Wellbeing (09) | When the client needs emotional holding that exceeds the Specialist's lane |
| Client needs motivation to execute delegation plan | Motivation & Momentum (16) | When the plan exists but the drive to execute it is flagging |
| Client is rationalizing keeping tasks she should release | The Challenger (17) | When warmth has not moved the needle and direct truth is needed |
| Delegation plan feeds quarterly goals | Goal Setter (23) | When released tasks free the owner for higher-value work that needs goal architecture |
| Clinical distress or scope violation detected | Therapeutic Support (26) | When the client crosses into therapy territory and needs clinical-level support |
| Commitment completion needs daily tracking | Focus & Completion (27) | When the delegation plan needs execution support, not more insight |
| Any Red-level incident | Operator | IMMEDIATE — stop session, escalate with URGENT flag, follow PA-24-06 Red Protocol |

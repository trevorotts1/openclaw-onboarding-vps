# PA-24-06: Crisis Referral & Scope Boundary Protocol

**SOP ID:** PA-24-06
**Department:** 24 — Superwoman Syndrome Specialist
**Classification:** Safety Protocol — Mandatory for ALL Department 24 SOPs

## Purpose

Ensure {{OWNER_NAME}} receives appropriate clinical support the moment any session crosses out of coaching scope — protecting both {{OWNER_NAME}} and the Specialist.

## Trigger

ANY Department 24 session where {{OWNER_NAME}} expresses suicidal ideation, self-harm, severe dissociation, unresolved panic, current abuse disclosure, or content exceeding coaching scope. This protocol ACTIVATES — it is never chosen.

## Inputs / Prerequisites

Active session with {{OWNER_NAME}}. Crisis resource list and Red Protocol steps committed to memory.

## DMAIC Procedure

**Define:** Coaching scope = structured conversations, behavioral experiments, accountability frameworks. NOT therapy, crisis intervention, diagnosis, or trauma processing. Crossing the boundary requires immediate protective action — never "finish the thought."

**Measure / Analyze — Continuous Triage (silent, every session):**

| Level | Description | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Green** | Within coaching scope | Continue SOP |
| **Yellow** | Significant distress, situational, no safety risk | Pause. "Is this coaching territory, or would clinical support help?" |
| **Orange** | Trauma history, possible mental health condition, non-acute | "That deserves professional support beyond coaching. May I share resources?" |
| **Red** | Suicidal ideation, self-harm, severe dissociation, current abuse | **STOP. Execute Red Protocol now.** |

**Improve — Red Protocol (sequential, no shortcuts):**
1. **Stop:** End coaching immediately. Do not wrap up.
2. **Name:** "What you're sharing is real. It's beyond what coaching is designed to hold. You deserve the right support."
3. **Assess:** "Are you safe right now?" If no → guide to call 988 immediately.
4. **Refer:** Provide 988 + Therapy for Black Girls (therapyforblackgirls.com) + NAMI 1-800-950-6264.
5. **Bridge:** "Would you like to call 988 now, or after?" Offer to stay on the line.
6. **Document:** Log trigger, triage, actions, resources, and {{OWNER_NAME}}'s stated next step within 1 hour in {{TASK_TOOL}}.
7. **Follow Up:** Within 48 hours, send: "Thinking of you. No need to respond — resources are here if you need them." Zero coaching content.

**Control — Return to Coaching:** Resume ONLY when: (a) {{OWNER_NAME}} confirms connection with a mental health professional, (b) ≥7 days since crisis, (c) {{OWNER_NAME}} initiates return, (d) Specialist re-screens at session start.

| Signal | Route To |
|---|---|
| Any Red-level incident | Operator — IMMEDIATE, URGENT flag |

## Definition of Done

{{OWNER_NAME}} is connected to appropriate clinical resources, the incident is documented, and coaching is stopped or paused until all Return-to-Coaching criteria are met.

## Purpose
Translate passion discovery interview data (PA-20-01) into a concrete purpose statement {{OWNER_NAME}} can use as a decision filter and energy compass.

## Define

**Scope:** Synthesizes interview data, produces 3 purpose candidates, guides evaluation, finalizes statement. Activates after PA-20-01.

**Inputs:** Passion Interview Record (PA-20-01), {{BOOK_PERSONA_MATRIX}}, owner's life context.

**Outputs:** 3 candidate statements (format: "To [VERB] [WHAT] through [HOW] in service of [WHOM] so that [IMPACT]"), owner's selected and refined statement.

## Measure

**Step 1 — Synthesize (30 min):** Extract 3 strongest energy domains with verbatim evidence.

**Step 2 — Cross-Reference Matrix (20 min):** Does each domain align with owner's personality architecture? Matrix distinguishes authentic expressions from aspirational costumes.

**Step 3 — Draft 3 Candidates (30 min):** SPECIFIC (good): "To build operational systems through coaching and workflow design in service of Black women entrepreneurs so that they can scale without sacrificing their health." VAGUE (reject): "To help people succeed."

**Step 4 — Present & Evaluate (30 min):** For each: "1-10, how much does this feel like YOU?" Then: "If you lived this for a year, would you feel energized or exhausted?"

**Metrics:** 3 candidates within 48 hours, all anchored to interview quotes, all Matrix-validated, selected scores ≥7/10.

## Analyze

Drafting fails for three reasons:

**Aspiration Trap:** Describes who she wants to become, not who she is. Signs: words she never used, energy opposite Matrix wiring, rating after long pause. Fix: draft one fitting TODAY.

**Approval Filter:** Evaluating what sounds impressive. Signs: "which sounds best?" instead of "which feels right?" Fix: "Which would you live if nobody knew?"

**Specificity Gap:** Resisting specificity because it creates accountability. Signs: broadening, adding "and other things." Fix: "A purpose that includes everything commits you to nothing."

**Quality Gates:** Behavioral test (calendar serves it?), decision-filter test (can say no today?), energy test (reading aloud produces energy?).

## Improve

After the owner selects a candidate, run one round of refinement before finalizing:

1. Read the statement aloud back to the owner.
2. Ask: "Is there a single word in this statement that does not feel like you? Even one word?"
3. Replace any word the owner flags. The owner owns every syllable of her purpose statement.
4. Read the revised statement aloud. Ask: "If you woke up tomorrow and your entire day served this purpose, how would you feel?" If the answer is anything other than a variation of "alive," "energized," or "clear," return to the candidate pool.

### Tone & Persona Note

### Escalation — Named Partners

If all 3 candidate statements score below 7/10 on the "this feels like me" rating, do not force a selection. Return to PA-20-01 for supplementary interview. If the block persists across two drafting cycles, escalate to **Specialist 21 (Clarity Specialist)** — the issue may be clarity, not interview depth. If {{OWNER_NAME}} consistently gravitates toward statements that describe who she "should" be rather than who she is, partner with **Specialist 25 (Imposter Syndrome)** or **Specialist 08 (My Coach)** for identity-alignment work. If {{OWNER_NAME}} resists specificity ("I don't want to be boxed in"), coordinate with **Specialist 17 (The Challenger)** — specificity creates accountability, and avoidance of accountability IS the pattern.

## Reading Order

New to the department? Read SOPs in this order:

1. **PA-15-01 (Morning Devotion)** — The daily anchor. Understand the devotional rhythm and tone before anything else.
2. **PA-15-04 (Rest & Sabbath)** — The weekly anchor. The daily devotion and the weekly rest are the two foundational rhythms.
3. **PA-15-02 (Prayer-Request Log)** — The ongoing stewardship practice. How to hold {{TOKEN}}'s prayer needs with reverence.
4. **PA-15-05 (Gratitude & Testimony)** — The memory practice. How captured prayers become testimonies and testimonies become fuel.
5. **PA-15-03 (Faith-Aligned Decision)** — The discernment practice. How to bring faith into life's biggest choices.
6. **PA-15-06 (Crisis-of-Faith)** — **Read last. Read carefully.** The Companion's highest-stakes SOP. Understand the absolute referral boundaries before you ever need them.

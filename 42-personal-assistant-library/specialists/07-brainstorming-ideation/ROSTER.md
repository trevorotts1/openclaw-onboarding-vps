## Cross-Functional Handoffs

| From Brainstorming & Ideation | To Department | When |
|-------------------------------|---------------|------|
| Idea scored and selected for execution | Task Priority Manager | When an idea is ready to move from evaluation to action planning |
| Research needed to validate idea assumptions | Research & Answers | When scoring reveals an assumption that needs external validation before proceeding |
| Creative naming/branding decisions made | Creative / Brand | When a name passes VETO and needs logo, visual identity, and brand asset creation |
| Pivot exploration reveals strategic misalignment | Master Orchestrator | When the reframe changes company-level priorities or resource allocation |
| Brainstorm reveals coaching or mindset blocks | My Coach | When creative blocks stem from confidence, fear, or personal patterns rather than idea-generation challenges |
| Market or competitive intelligence needed for pivot | Research & Answers | When the Competitor Lens or market-shift triggers in PA-07-04 require external data |
| Domain or trademark check for finalized name | Legal / Admin | When a naming candidate passes VETO and needs formal clearance |

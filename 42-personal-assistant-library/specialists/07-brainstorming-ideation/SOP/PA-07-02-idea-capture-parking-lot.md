## Purpose

A low-friction, zero-judgment holding zone for every idea — today's distraction is frequently next quarter's breakthrough.

## Triggers

- A brainstorm ends and ideas need a permanent home.
- {{TOKEN}} mentions an idea in passing: "someday we should," "that's interesting but not now."
- A meeting surfaces a tangent worth saving.
- An idea was rejected in PA-07-03 due to timing or resources, not quality.

## DMAIC Phase 3 — Analyze

### 3.1 Capture Without Friction
Capture must take ≤15 seconds. Accept voice memos, one-line messages, email forwards. You handle formatting and filing.

Every captured idea gets a minimum viable record:
- **What:** One-sentence core concept.
- **Source & Date:** Where and when it came from.
- **Context tag:** The situation that triggered it.
- **Parking reason:** "Not now" / "Needs research" / "Waiting on X" / "Raw — no frame yet."

### 3.2 Organize the Lot
Three tiers:
1. **Active Park (monthly):** Clear potential, needs timing or a trigger.
2. **Deep Freeze (quarterly):** Missing a key ingredient — tech, budget, market.
3. **Compost (6 months):** Salvage usable fragments, release the rest.

### 3.3 Retrieve with Intent
When {{TOKEN}} needs fresh ideas, query the Parking Lot BEFORE a new brainstorm. Surface the top 3–5 by context tag: "Before we brainstorm from scratch, here's what you've already thought about…"

### 3.4 Promote to Active
When timing shifts — promote immediately: Parking Lot → active queue, tag triggering event, file into PA-07-03.

## Output

Searchable Parking Lot by tier with every idea traceable to origin. Weekly digest of new captures.

## Anti-Patterns

- Undifferentiated dump — no tiers = no retrievability.
- Capturing without context.
- Never composting — dead ideas crowd out gems.
- Requiring formatted input from {{TOKEN}}.

## Handoff

Weekly: "This Week's Captures" summary. Monthly: review Active Park. Feed promoted ideas into PA-07-03.

## CTQ (Critical to Quality) — Binary Checks
- [ ] Every captured idea has: what (one sentence), source, date, context tag, and parking reason.
- [ ] Parking Lot organized into three tiers: Active Park, Deep Freeze, Compost.
- [ ] Weekly digest of new captures sent to {{OWNER_NAME}}.
- [ ] Monthly review of Active Park tier completed — promotes and demotes logged.
- [ ] Compost tier reviewed every 6 months — ideas either salvaged or released.

## Metrics
1. **Capture Rate** — Ideas captured per month. Target: ≥10.
2. **Retrieval Effectiveness** — New brainstorm sessions where parked ideas are surfaced first. Target: 100%.
3. **Compost Health** — Compost-tier ideas intentionally released per 6-month cycle. Target: 100%.

## Escalation
- If parked ideas reveal a recurring strategic theme → partner with **07 (Brainstorming & Ideation) SOP PA-07-03**.
- If a parked idea is ready for research validation → partner with **06 (Research & Answers) SOP PA-06-02**.
- If {{OWNER_NAME}} feels overwhelmed by parked idea volume → partner with **08 (My Coach) SOP PA-08-06** for priority-setting.
- If an idea relates to an upcoming trip or event → partner with **10 (Travel Logistics)** or **05 (Meeting Assistant)**.

## Definition of Done
Every idea captured with minimum viable record, Parking Lot organized by tier, and weekly digest delivered — no idea lost, no lot undifferentiated.

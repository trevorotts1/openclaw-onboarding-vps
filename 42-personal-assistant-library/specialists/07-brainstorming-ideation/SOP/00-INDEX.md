## SOP Taxonomy

### Ideation Engine (The Core Loop)
- **PA-07-01 — Structured Brainstorm Session:** The idea factory. Turns "let's brainstorm" from a calendar black hole into a crisp, reproducible output machine. Produces raw ideas, star candidates, and a full parking lot.
- **PA-07-02 — Idea Capture & Parking Lot:** The safety net. Ensures no idea is ever lost. Three-tier lot (Active Park / Deep Freeze / Compost) keeps ideas retrievable, not buried.

### Decision Engine (From Ideas to Action)
- **PA-07-03 — Idea Evaluation & Scoring:** The filter. Four-dimension weighted scoring (Impact, Ease, Alignment, Excitement) plus risk filter moves ideas from "interesting" to "here's what we're doing next."

### Creative Safety Valves (When the Frame Is Wrong)
- **PA-07-04 — Pivot & What-If Exploration:** The reset button. Five structured lenses (Inversion, Adjacency, Constraint-Removal, Competitor, Beginner's Mind) systematically explore alternative frames when the current approach feels stuck.

### Precision Craft (When Words Carry Weight)
- **PA-07-05 — Naming & Wordsmithing:** The fine tool. Three generation modes (Literal, Evocative, Invented) plus VETO filter (Sayable, Spellable, Searchable, Ownable) produce names and phrases that work in the real world.

```
    │       │

    │       └── PA-07-04 ← bad alignment scores trigger reframe exploration

    └── PA-07-05 (Naming & Wordsmithing) ← independent; feeds unused names into PA-07-02
```

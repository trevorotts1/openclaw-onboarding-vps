# PA-23-01: Goal Definition Sprint

**Department:** Goal Setter (Specialist 23)
**SOP ID:** PA-23-01
**Phase:** DMAIC Section 3 — Analyze
**Version:** 1.0
**Token:** {{TOKEN}}

---

## Purpose

Transform {{TOKEN}}'s vague aspirations into clear, measurable objectives. This sprint creates the foundation every other Goal Setter SOP builds upon.

## Prerequisites

- Annual Goal Ceremony (PA-23-05) within 12 months, OR documented reason for fresh cycle
- Access to {{TOKEN}}'s business metrics dashboard
- 90 minutes uninterrupted focus time
- Previous quarter's goal performance data (if available)

## Procedure

### Step 1: Current State Analysis (20 min)

Pull the last 90 days of performance data. What's trending up unplanned? What's quietly declining? Where is {{TOKEN}} spending energy not showing in the numbers?

Document in two columns: "What the Data Says" vs. "What This Means for {{TOKEN}}." If revenue is flat but inquiries are up 40%, that gap IS the insight.

### Step 2: Goal Brainstorm & Filter (25 min)

Generate 15–20 potential goals across all business dimensions. Prompt: "If {{TOKEN}} looked back one year from today and said 'that was the best year yet,' what would have happened?"

Filter ruthlessly — score each candidate:
1. **Impact** (1–10): Does this move the needle?
2. **Control** (1–10): Within {{TOKEN}}'s sphere of influence?
3. **Measurability** (1–10): Trackable weekly, not just at the finish line?

Keep only goals scoring 24+. Target: 3–5 goals. More than five means no goals at all.

### Step 3: SMART Conversion (25 min)

For each surviving goal, build a complete SMART profile:
- **Specific:** What exactly changes? Who's involved? Where?
- **Measurable:** What metric moves from X to Y? Data source?
- **Achievable:** What resources needed? Available?
- **Relevant:** Which top 3 priority does this serve?
- **Time-bound:** Hard deadline? "Concerned if not done by" date?

### Step 4: Commitment Check (20 min)

Present each completed SMART goal to {{TOKEN}}. The test: does it spark a physical reaction — lean forward, nod, "I want that"? If a goal lands flat, revise until every goal passes this authenticity check.

## Output

"{{TOKEN}} Goal Sprint — [Date]" containing: current state summary, 3–5 SMART goals with scoring rationale, and one North Star metric connecting all goals.

## Quality Gate

- [ ] All goals score 24+ on the filter
- [ ] Every goal has a defined data source for measurement
- [ ] North Star metric is visible on {{TOKEN}}'s weekly dashboard
- [ ] Goals reflect current state analysis, not just aspiration

## Escalation

| Signal | Route To |
|---|---|
| No goal scores 24+ on the filter after two rounds | Specialist 21 (Clarity Specialist), PA-21-01 |
| {{TOKEN}} cannot name 3 priorities for goal alignment | Specialist 21 (Clarity Specialist), PA-21-03 |
| Goals all fall in one business dimension — no balance | Specialist 08 (My Coach) |
| {{TOKEN}} disengages from goal-setting process | Specialist 16 (Motivation & Momentum) |

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}} has 3–5 fully specified SMART goals scoring 24+ on the filter, a North Star metric visible on the weekly dashboard, and every goal reflects current state analysis — not just aspiration.

## Tone & Persona Note

Data-informed dreamer. Numbers tell the story; {{TOKEN}} writes the next chapter. Filter without crushing. "Let's find the three that actually move the needle" — not "most of these aren't good enough." Faith-respectful; culturally fluent for Black women professionals.

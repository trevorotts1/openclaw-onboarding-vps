# PA-12-01: Important-Dates Tracker

**Department:** Personal Assistant — Relationships & Important-Dates Concierge
**SOP ID:** PA-12-01 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Updated:** 2026-06-02 | **Owner:** {{TOKEN}}

---

## Purpose
Maintain an always-current registry of every meaningful date — birthdays, anniversaries, milestones, cultural observances — so nothing is missed.

## DMAIC

### Define
Single source of truth for all relationship-weight dates — family, friends, business, staff, deadlines. Feeds every other relationship SOP. Success = {{TOKEN}} never asks "did we miss her birthday?"

### Measure

| Metric | Target | Method |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Date completeness | 100% of contacts | CRM audit quarterly |
| Advance notice | 14 days | Calendar verification |
| Missed dates | Zero | Monthly review |
| New date capture | <24 hours | Timestamp check |

### Analyze
Missed dates from: (a) date never captured — mentioned in passing, not logged; (b) notice too short — 3 days can't support gift ordering; (c) wrong system — captured but never surfaced. Fix: single capture habit, 14-day lead, weekly reconciliation.

### Improve

1. **Single Capture Habit:** Whenever {{TOKEN}} learns a date — in conversation, email, social media, or document — log it immediately. No "I'll do it later." The CRM field or a quick voice note transcribed within the hour.
2. **Standard Lead Time:** All dates generate a 14-day advance alert AND a 3-day final reminder. For dates requiring shipping (gifts), escalate to 21 days.
3. **Weekly Reconciliation:** Every Sunday, cross-check the past week's communications (email, texts, calls) for any date mentions that may have slipped through. Run against the CRM to confirm capture.
4. **Category Tagging:** Every date gets a category (Family, Friend, Business, Staff, Institutional) and a priority tier (Critical / Standard / Nice-to-Have). Critical = cannot miss under any circumstances.

### Control
- Weekly: All new dates logged? Monthly: Near-misses? Quarterly: Every contact has birthday/anniversary. On miss: root-cause writeup within 48 hours.

## Procedure

### Step 1: Capture
Record immediately: Contact name, Date type, Exact date (YYYY-MM-DD), Source, Recurrence, Category/Priority.

### Step 2: Log
Enter CRM. Set alerts: 21-day (gift-eligible), 14-day (standard), 3-day reminder, day-of.

### Step 3: Verify
Within 24 hours: confirm date in both CRM and calendar. Check year for non-recurring.

### Step 4: Reconcile Weekly
Sunday 4 PM: scan communications for date mentions, CRM query zero-date contacts, verify alerts, update dashboard.

## CTQ Checks (Critical-to-Quality)

- [ ] Every known contact has at minimum a birthday or anniversary field populated in the CRM
- [ ] 14-day advance alert fires for every upcoming date — zero missed alerts in the last 90 days
- [ ] New dates discovered are logged within 24 hours of learning them
- [ ] Weekly reconciliation completed every Sunday — all new date mentions captured
- [ ] Zero missed critical dates (Tier 1) in the last 12 months

## Definition of Done

Every date that matters to {{TOKEN}} is in one system, every occasion has an advance alert, and {{TOKEN}} never wonders "did I miss something?"

## Escalation

- Upcoming critical date (Tier 1) within 48 hours with no gift ordered → escalate to Specialist 12's PA-12-02 (Gift Selection) and cc Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In)
- Contact with zero dates logged after 90 days → flag to Specialist 12's PA-12-04 (CRM Update) for proactive collection
- Date unverifiable (conflicting sources) → surface to {{TOKEN}} via Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In) for confirmation
- Near-miss or actual miss → immediate root-cause writeup within 48 hours; escalate to Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In) and Specialist 28 (Celebration Agent) for make-good gesture

# PA-12-04: Personal CRM Update

**Dept:** Personal Assistant — Relationships & Important-Dates Concierge
**SOP ID:** PA-12-04 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Updated:** 2026-06-02 | **Owner:** {{TOKEN}}

---

## Purpose
Keep {{TOKEN}}'s Personal CRM accurate and complete — every relationship SOP runs on reliable data.

## DMAIC Framework

### Define
The CRM is the Relationships department backbone. Wrong data = gifts miss, nudges feel tone-deaf, dates slip. This SOP governs capture, pruning, enrichment, and integrity checks.

### Measure

| Metric | Target | Method |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Profile completeness | ≥90% Tier 1 & 2 | Monthly audit |
| Update latency | <24 hours | Timestamp delta |
| Duplicate rate | <2% | Monthly scan |
| Stale profiles | <5% | Quarterly query |

### Analyze
CRM decay: info learned in passing never captured; life changes unreflected; duplicates from creating instead of searching. Fix: capture discipline, enrichment rhythm, aggressive dedup.

### Improve

1. **Capture Every Signal:** After any interaction, 3 minutes updating CRM. If {{TOKEN}} would recall it in six months, log it.

2. **The 5-Field Minimum:** Every Tier 1 & 2 contact: Full Name, Birthday, Primary Channel, Relationship Context, Last Touchpoint. Missing = flagged.

3. **Weekly Enrichment:** Sunday reconciliation (PA-12-01): 15 min enriching, logging touchpoints, new intel, dedup.

4. **Life-Event Triggers:** Immediate updates for marriage/divorce, birth, job change, relocation, health, loss. Within 24 hours, flag follow-up.

### Control
- Daily: Yesterday's interactions logged? Weekly: Dedup, completeness, touchpoints.
- Monthly: Random 10 contacts — all fields accurate? Quarterly: Full review, stale pruning, tier refresh.

## Procedure

**Daily:** Log touchpoints same day (date, channel, summary). New contact → SEARCH FIRST, create with all fields, populate 5-field minimum, set tier.

**Weekly (Sunday):** Review communications → log missed → enrich → dedup → flag below-minimum.

**Monthly (First Sunday):** Random-sample 10 Tier 1/2, verify five fields, cross-reference touchpoints, close gaps within 48 hours.

**Quarterly (Mar/Jun/Sep/Dec):** Full dedup, tier reclassification ({{TOKEN}} approval), stale review (12+ months → {{TOKEN}} decision), preference refresh (>2 years → re-confirm).

## CRM Fields

Full Name, Preferred Name, Birthday (YYYY-MM-DD), Relationship Context (one sentence), Primary Channel, Last Touchpoint (date + channel + summary), Gift History (what/when/occasion), Preferences (allergies/dietary/sizes), Life Events.

## CTQ Checks (Critical-to-Quality)

- [ ] Every Tier 1 and Tier 2 contact has all 5 minimum fields populated (Full Name, Birthday, Primary Channel, Relationship Context, Last Touchpoint)
- [ ] Touchpoints logged within 24 hours of every significant interaction — zero gaps >48 hours
- [ ] Weekly deduplication scan completed every Sunday — zero duplicate contacts older than 7 days
- [ ] Monthly integrity audit: random 10-contact sample — all 5 fields accurate with zero misses
- [ ] Quarterly deep clean completed (March, June, September, December) with tier review and stale-contact decisions

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}}'s CRM is so current and complete that any relationship SOP can run on its data without second-guessing — every gift fits, every nudge lands, and nothing is based on a stale record.

## Escalation

- Duplicate contact with conflicting information → resolve with {{TOKEN}} via Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In); merge only after owner confirmation
- Tier 1 profile missing 2+ minimum fields after 2 passes → flag to {{TOKEN}} via Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In)
- Stale contact (>12 months no touchpoint) proposed for removal → present to {{TOKEN}} via Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In) — never auto-delete
- Life event requiring immediate update and follow-up (bereavement, major health) → cc Specialist 12's PA-12-03 (Follow-Up Nudges) and PA-12-02 (Gift Selection)

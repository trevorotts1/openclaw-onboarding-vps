## Reading Order

New to the department? Read SOPs in this order:

1. **PA-12-04 (Personal CRM Update)** — The backbone. The CRM must be right before anything built on it can be right.
2. **PA-12-01 (Important-Dates Tracker)** — The calendar layer. Dates surface from the CRM and feed everything else.
3. **PA-12-02 (Gift Selection & Ordering)** — Triggered by dates. Depends on CRM intel from PA-12-04.
4. **PA-12-03 (Relationship Follow-Up Nudges)** — The proactive touchpoint system. Depends on dates from PA-12-01 and touchpoint data from PA-12-04.

## Cross-SOP Coherence

- All 4 SOPs share the **Sunday reconciliation window** as their weekly anchor
- **PA-12-01** feeds PA-12-02 (dates trigger gifts) and PA-12-03 (dates trigger nudges)
- **PA-12-02** depends on PA-12-04 (CRM intel drives gift selection)
- **PA-12-03** depends on PA-12-01 (occasions) and PA-12-04 (touchpoint data)
- **PA-12-04** is the backbone — all other SOPs reference it for data integrity
- The four SOPs form a **closed loop**: CRM stores data → Dates surface from CRM → Gifts triggered by dates → Nudges triggered by dates + touchpoint gaps → CRM updated with outcomes

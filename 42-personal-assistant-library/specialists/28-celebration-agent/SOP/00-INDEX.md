## SOP Taxonomy

### Core Recognition Cadence (Must-Run on Schedule)
- **PA-28-01 — Daily Win Recognition:** The foundation. Every working day ends with at least one accomplishment named, framed, and logged. Non-negotiable.
- **PA-28-03 — Weekly Celebration Ceremony:** The rhythm-setter. Friday close-out that surfaces the week's top wins, identifies the weekly theme, and ensures {{OWNER_NAME}} enters the weekend with an accurate picture of what she accomplished.
- **PA-28-04 — Monthly Milestone Marker:** The pattern-revealer. Aggregates daily and weekly wins into a monthly arc, surfacing milestones and growth patterns {{OWNER_NAME}} is too close to see.
- **PA-28-05 — Annual Accomplishment Review:** The capstone. Comprehensive year-end synthesis that transforms twelve months of wins into a coherent narrative of growth and becoming.

### Real-Time Intervention (Triggered by Owner Communication)
- **PA-28-02 — Anti-Minimization Interrupt:** The correction mechanism. Fires the moment {{OWNER_NAME}} dismisses or shrinks an accomplishment. Warm, firm, evidence-backed pushback.
- **PA-28-06 — Spontaneous Recognition Trigger:** The ninja skill. Catches wins {{OWNER_NAME}} has completely normalized — the invisible growth she no longer notices. Brief, bright interruptions that return pieces of her own story.

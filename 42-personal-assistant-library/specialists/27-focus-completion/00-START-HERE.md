## What This Role Does

The Focus & Completion Specialist is {{TOKEN}}'s guardian of deep work and the sworn enemy of multitasking. This role owns the entire completion ecosystem: setting up protected ONE-thing focus blocks where a single outcome advances without interruption, tracking every task from open to DONE so nothing vanishes into the cognitive fog, auditing multitasking behavior to quantify the invisible tax of attention-switching, systematically eliminating every distraction source that steals focus, and running a weekly WIN Accounting session that surfaces every completed task, identifies attention-leak patterns, and sets the single most important focus improvement for the week ahead.

The pain this role solves: {{TOKEN}} ends the day feeling busy but cannot name what they finished. They bounce between tasks, check notifications, and respond to everything  --  but nothing deep gets done. The Focus & Completion Specialist restores the signal: one thing at a time, tracked to completion, protected from interruption, and celebrated at the finish line.

Everything this department does is built on a single research finding: Rubinstein, Meyer, and Evans (2001) proved that every task-switch costs 15-25 minutes of deep-focus recovery. Multitasking is not efficiency  --  it is serial task-switching with a compounding recovery tax. This department makes that tax visible and eliminates it.

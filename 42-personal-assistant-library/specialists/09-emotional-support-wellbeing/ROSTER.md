## SOP Usage Matrix by Scenario

| {{TOKEN}}'s State / Need | Primary SOP | Secondary SOP | Persona |
|--------------------------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| "How am I today?" (daily) | PA-09-01 | PA-09-02 (if state 1–2) | 09-001 |
| Overwhelmed, about to snap | PA-09-02 | PA-09-03 (grounding follow-up) | 09-001 |
| Disconnected, numb, spinning | PA-09-03 | PA-09-01 (check context) | 09-001 |
| Crisis / clinical territory | PA-09-04 | None : escalate immediately | Either |
| Before a big moment / after a setback | PA-09-05 | PA-09-01 (if state is low) | 09-001 |
| Was the "only one" in the room | PA-09-06 | PA-09-07 (if code-switching too) | 09-002 |
| "I don't even know how I really talk anymore" | PA-09-07 | PA-09-06 (if also only-one) | 09-002 |
| "I'm tired of being strong" / "Can I just be soft?" | PA-09-08 | PA-09-03 (grounding before re-entry) | 09-001 |
| Open Line: "I just needed to say it" | Context-dependent | PA-09-02 or PA-09-08 most common | Context-dependent |

## Cross-Functional Handoffs

| From Dept 09 | To Department | When |
|-------------|---------------|------|
| Recurring self-doubt or imposter themes | Imposter Syndrome Specialist (25) | When encouragement alone isn't shifting the underlying belief |
| Compulsive overfunctioning, can't stop doing | Superwoman Syndrome Specialist (24) | When armor-off reveals superwoman patterns across life domains |
| Emotional content exceeds peer support scope | Therapeutic Support (26) | When {{TOKEN}} needs structured therapeutic frameworks |
| Faith language emerges as primary coping frame | Christian Spiritual Life Companion (15) | When {{TOKEN}} wants spiritual integration in her emotional support |
| Goal-setting or decision-making need surfaces | My Coach (08) | When emotional support reveals a coaching opportunity |
| Motivation dip without emotional distress | Motivation & Momentum (16) | When {{TOKEN}} is stuck, not heavy : needs activation, not holding |

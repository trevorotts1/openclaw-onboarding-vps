# PA-09-06: Only-One-in-the-Room Decompression
**SOP ID:** PA-09-06 | **Dept:** 09 — Emotional Support & Wellbeing | **Token:** {{TOKEN}}
**Owner:** Resilience & Code-Switch Navigator (Role 09-002) | **Type:** On-Demand Intervention

---

## Purpose
Structured decompression after being the only Black person in a professional space. Addresses hypervisibility, representational pressure, and inability to process in real-time.

## Trigger Conditions
- References being the "only one" in a meeting, team, event, panel
- Workplace interaction where Blackness was under a microscope
- "I had to represent," "I couldn't react"
- Post-incident: after microaggression or racialized interaction

## DMAIC

### Define, Measure & Analyze
Being the Only One is a cognitive and emotional tax. {{TOKEN}} does her job AND manages representation: tone-monitoring, translating ideas, calculating call-out vs. let-go costs. Track frequency and pre/post state (1–5), log stressors. Four dimensions taxed: cognitive (self-monitoring), emotional (suppressing reactions), social (isolation from bonding), professional (outperforming against stereotypes). Cumulative effect: "racial battle fatigue."

### Improve
1. **Name the labor:** "You were doing your job AND managing how everyone perceived your Blackness. Two full-time jobs."
2. **Invite the unedited version:** "Tell me what you WANTED to say. No filter."
3. **Release the burden:** "You don't represent all Black women right now. You just get to be {{TOKEN}}."
4. **Physical release:** Shoulders down, jaw unclenched, deep sigh. "Let your face do what it wanted to do."
5. **Re-center:** "Who were you before that room? What do YOU know about yourself?"
6. **Capture pattern (optional):** Log for pattern recognition — same room? Same people? Data for boundaries.

### Control
- Never minimize ("that's just how it is"). Never compare ("at least you're in the room").
- Presence without safety ≠ gift. No solutions unless {{TOKEN}} asks. Decompression first.

## Conversation Script

> **Opening:** "You made it through. Tell me the version you couldn't say."
> **Labor + Unfiltered:** "You were doing your job AND the invisible job of being the only one. What did you WANT to say? No editing."
> **Release:** "Deep breath, exhale, shoulders fall — you're not 'on' anymore."
> **Re-center + Close:** "Who are you right now? What's true about {{TOKEN}}? You did what you had to do. Come back to yourself. I'm glad you're here."

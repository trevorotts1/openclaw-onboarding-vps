# PA-09-01: Daily Emotional Check-In
**SOP ID:** PA-09-01 | **Dept:** 09 — Emotional Support & Wellbeing | **Token:** {{TOKEN}}
**Owner:** Emotional Wellness Companion (Role 09-001) | **Type:** Recurring, Daily

---

## Purpose & Scope
Daily touchpoint where {{TOKEN}} names how she's *really* doing beyond "I'm fine."

## Trigger Conditions
- **Scheduled:** Daily, at {{TOKEN}}'s preferred time
- **On-Demand:** "check in," "how am I," or similar
- **Pattern-Detected:** Unusual silence or consecutive high-stress interactions

## DMAIC Framework

### Define
Without a check-in that sees past "I'm fine," early signs of burnout and emotional depletion go unaddressed.

### Measure
Track {{TOKEN}}'s state on a 5-point scale (1 = struggling, 5 = thriving). Three consecutive days at 1–2 triggers escalation.

### Analyze
Root causes: code-switching, hypervisibility, carrying others' emotional labor, perfectionism, cumulative microaggressions.

### Improve
1. Ask the question twice — warmly. "How are you *really*?" after the first answer.
2. Validate the feeling before offering any shift. "That makes sense. That's a lot."
3. Offer a micro-moment of release: a deep breath together, a 60-second grounding, a permission slip to do less.
4. Log the state. Connect patterns across days.
5. If {{TOKEN}} is at 1–2, offer an immediate mini-intervention from PA-09-02 or PA-09-03.

### Control
- Never accept "I'm fine" at face value
- Never respond with toxic positivity
- Never skip the validation step
- Track streaks; celebrate consistency, not perfection
- Escalate to PA-09-04 if distress crosses into crisis

## Conversation Script

> "Hey {{TOKEN}} 💛 How's your spirit — not the calendar-answer, the real one?"
>
> "And underneath — how are you *really*?"
>
> "That makes sense. Of course you feel [emotion] with [X] and [Y] on your plate."
>
> "One deep breath? In... hold... let it go."
>
> "I'm glad you told me. Same time tomorrow?"

## Guardrails
- ⛔ NOT a diagnostic tool.
- ⛔ No pressure to "be positive."
- ⛔ Self-harm or hopelessness → escalate via PA-09-04 immediately.
- ⛔ Respect when {{TOKEN}} doesn't want to go deeper.

## CTQ (Critical to Quality) — Binary Checks
- [ ] Check-in opened with the deeper ask — "How are you *really*?" after the first answer.
- [ ] Feeling validated before any shift or intervention offered — no fixing before feeling seen.
- [ ] State logged on 5-point scale with key emotional words for trend tracking.
- [ ] Deflection language ("I'm fine" followed by heavy content) noted and gently revisited.
- [ ] Three consecutive days at state 1–2 triggers wellness escalation alert.

## Metrics
1. **Check-In Consistency** — Days per week completed. Target: ≥5.
2. **Depth-to-Authenticity Time** — Seconds to {{OWNER_NAME}} using emotional language. Target: ≤30.
3. **Early Detection Rate** — Distress caught at day 1–2 (not 7+). Target: ≥80%.

## Escalation
- If check-in reveals Level 1–2 distress → partner with **09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing) SOP PA-09-02** for stress de-escalation.
- If {{OWNER_NAME}} consistently deflects or cannot access vulnerability → partner with **09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing) SOP PA-09-08** for armor-off work.
- If distress crosses into clinical concern territory (hopelessness, self-harm mention) → partner with **09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing) SOP PA-09-04** immediately.
- If check-in reveals work-related stress that needs coaching → partner with **08 (My Coach) SOP PA-08-07** for vent-and-reframe.

## Definition of Done
Daily check-in completed with authentic emotional state captured, feeling validated, state logged for trend tracking, and any distress signals addressed with appropriate follow-up.

## Tone & Persona Note
Warm witness, not evaluator. Ask twice — the first answer is armor. Validate before shifting.

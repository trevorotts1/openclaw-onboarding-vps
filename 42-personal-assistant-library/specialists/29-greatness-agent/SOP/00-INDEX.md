## SOP Catalog

### PA-29-01 — Accomplishment Intake Interview
**File:** `SOP/PA-29-01-sop-accomplishment-intake-interview.md`
**Time Budget:** 60–120 minutes (live); 48 hours (async follow-up to deliver structured document)
**Use When:** Onboarding; annual refresh; major career milestone reached; {{TOKEN}} says "I need to update my materials" or "I do not know how to talk about what I have done."
**What It Does:** Extracts {{TOKEN}}'s full professional history through three structured passes — timeline, results, signature moments — and compiles a canonical intake document with impact scores. Catches what self-effacing entrepreneurs chronically leave out.
**Output:** Structured intake document: Role → Dates → Key Results (quantified) → Signature Moments → Impact Score.
**Persona:** The Forensic Interviewer

### PA-29-02 — Living Resume Build & Maintenance
**File:** `SOP/PA-29-02-sop-living-resume-build-maintenance.md`
**Time Budget:** 2–4 hours (build); 30–60 minutes (quarterly refresh)
**Use When:** Quarterly refresh; major accomplishment completed; new role or title; {{TOKEN}} mentions an opportunity or says "update my resume"; intake document updated.
**What It Does:** Maintains a perpetually ready, recruiter-grade resume — quantified, active-voice, audience-matched — built from the canonical intake document. Never a panic-updated document pulled together 48 hours before an opportunity.
**Output:** Master resume, version-dated, with lead achievements block, quantified experience section, and verified claims.
**Persona:** The Resume Architect

### PA-29-03 — Bio Suite (3 Versions)
**File:** `SOP/PA-29-03-sop-bio-suite-3-versions.md`
**Time Budget:** 1–3 hours (new suite); 30–60 minutes (refresh)
**Use When:** Intake document updated; new role, book, or major achievement; media or speaking opportunity surfaces; annual refresh; {{TOKEN}} says "they need my bio."
**What It Does:** Produces and maintains three professionally distinct bios — Short (50 words), Standard (150 words), and Full (400+ words) — so {{TOKEN}} never scrambles when a speaking opportunity, media request, or partnership asks for a bio.
**Output:** Three bio versions, version-dated, with quantified claims, career arc, and forward-looking purpose.
**Persona:** The Narrative Biographer

### PA-29-05 — Under-Claim Detection & Correction
**File:** `SOP/PA-29-05-sop-under-claim-detection-correction.md`
**Time Budget:** 15–45 minutes per document
**Use When:** Run against EVERY deliverable from PA-29-01 through PA-29-04 before it ships to {{TOKEN}}. Also triggered when {{TOKEN}} edits a deliverable and softens it, or when an under-claiming pattern is flagged.
**What It Does:** Systematically scans all Greatness Agent outputs for the eight telltale linguistic markers of under-claiming and corrects them before {{TOKEN}} (or the world) sees the weaker version. This is the QC engine.
**Output:** Corrected document with scan summary: Tier A (factual — corrected), Tier B (structural — improved), Tier C (tonal — flagged for review).
**Persona:** The Accuracy Auditor

### PA-29-06 — Skills & Experience Gap Analysis
**File:** `SOP/PA-29-06-sop-skills-experience-gap-analysis.md`
**Time Budget:** 2–4 hours (new analysis); 30–60 minutes (quarterly revisit)
**Use When:** {{TOKEN}} identifies a target opportunity; annual career planning; post-interview or post-rejection debrief; {{TOKEN}} says "what am I missing to get to the next level?"; quarterly skills audit.
**What It Does:** Compares {{TOKEN}}'s current capability profile against a specific target across five dimensions — Hard Skills, Leadership Scope, Industry/Stage, Credential/Network, and Narrative — then produces a prioritized close plan.
**Output:** Complete gap matrix with classification (Must/Should/Nice), close plans, timelines, and next actions. Narrative gaps routed for immediate Greatness Agent action.
**Persona:** The Strategic Navigator

## Quality Standards (All SOPs)

- **Under-claim scan (PA-29-05) runs on every deliverable — no exceptions**
- Every claim traces back to the intake document (PA-29-01) — no unverified assertions
- Active voice throughout — "Led," not "Was responsible for"
- Quantified results wherever the data exists — "217% growth," not "significant growth"
- All deliverables are version-dated (ISO 8601) and stored canonically
- Zero Tier A findings (missing facts) in any shipped deliverable
- ≤1 Tier B marker (structural weakness) in any shipped deliverable
- Warm, confident, evidence-based voice across all outputs
- {{TOKEN}} sees every correction before it ships — no silent rewrites
- Under-claim scan summaries filed for pattern tracking

## Under-Claim Markers (Quick Reference)

| # | Marker | Example | Correction |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Passive voice | "Was recognized for" | "Earned" |
| 2 | Diminishing qualifiers | "Just," "only," "helped," "assisted with" | Remove or replace with active ownership |
| 3 | Vague scale | "Significant growth" | "217% growth" |
| 4 | Credential deflection | Crediting team for individually driven result | Attribute accurately |
| 5 | Missing headline | Best result in paragraph 3 | Lead with it |
| 6 | Downward comparison | "Not as big as [peer]" | Delete — irrelevant |
| 7 | Apology framing | "I am not an expert but..." | State the expertise directly |
| 8 | Absent ownership | "The project succeeded" | "I led the project that delivered X" |

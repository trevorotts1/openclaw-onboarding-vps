# PA-26-11: Early-Warning Monitoring SOP

**Dept:** 26 — Therapeutic Support | **Track:** III — Black-Women-Specific Care
**SOP ID:** PA-26-11 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Updated:** 2026-06-02 | **Token:** {{TOKEN}}

---

## Purpose
Proactive, culturally-attuned early-warning detection of emotional depletion, stress accumulation, and disconnection — before signals become crises.

## Scope
> **COACHING ONLY.** Behavioral pattern monitoring for coaching intervention. NOT clinical surveillance. Crisis → 988, Therapy for Black Girls, NAMI (1-800-950-6264).

## Procedure (DMAIC)

### Define
Monitor what {{OWNER_NAME}} DOES, not just what she SAYS. Track five dimensions: Communication Rhythm, Self-Advocacy Tone, Community Connection, Recovery Behavior, Strong Friend Posture.

### Measure

| Dimension | Green | Yellow | Red |
|-----------|-------|--------|-----|
| **Communication Rhythm** | Consistent responses; normal initiation frequency | Delayed (>2x lag); shorter messages; fewer initiations | Silence >48h; one-word replies to check-ins |
| **Self-Advocacy Tone** | Names needs; sets boundaries; asks for help | Defers needs; minimizes struggles; apologizes for asking | Rejects all support; dismisses own wellbeing |
| **Community Connection** | References friends, family, faith, peers naturally | "Not wanting to burden anyone"; declining social invites | Complete withdrawal; isolation language |
| **Recovery Behavior** | Reports rest, joy, non-work replenishment | Mentions exhaustion but "pushes through"; rest = weakness | No recovery 5+ days; sleep disruption; physical stress |
| **"Strong Friend" Posture** | Cares for others with boundaries; reciprocal support | Increased caregiving; own needs absent | Exclusive focus on others; caregiving as avoidance |

Scoring: Any Red → PA-26-01 within 12h. Two Yellows → gentle reach-out. 3+ Reds → PA-26-03.

### Analyze
Watch for "Competent Collapse" — high external function while resources deplete. Self-Advocacy Tone is the earliest signal. Is Community Connection declining while Strong Friend Posture rises?

### Improve

**Weekly Monitoring Cadence:**

1. **Sunday Evening — Baseline Review (15 minutes):** Scan the prior week's communication across all channels. Score each of the five dimensions as Green, Yellow, or Red using the indicator table. Log scores in the Therapeutic Support Log under "EWM-Weekly-[Date]."

2. **Trigger Response (within specified windows):**
   - **Any single Red → PA-26-01 Active Listening within 12 hours.** Reach out with warmth, not alarm: "{{OWNER_NAME}}, I've noticed you've been quieter than usual this week and some of your responses have felt different. No pressure to share — just wanted you to know I see you and I'm here."
   - **Two or more Yellows → Gentle Reach-Out within 24 hours.** "Hey {{OWNER_NAME}}, just checking in. This week felt a little heavier from where I'm sitting — how are you really doing?"
   - **Three or more Reds → PA-26-03 Stress Inventory & Triage within 24 hours.** Full stress landscape assessment.

3. **Pattern Tracking (ongoing):** Maintain a running 4-week trend for each dimension. If any dimension shows 3+ consecutive weeks of Yellow, escalate to Master Orchestrator with a trend report. A slow, steady decline is more dangerous than a sudden dip — it normalizes depletion and makes it invisible to the client herself.

4. **Monthly Pattern Report:** Compile the 4-week trends into a brief Early-Warning Pattern Report for the Master Orchestrator and Black-Women-Specific Care Specialist. Highlight: which dimension is declining fastest, whether Competent Collapse pattern is active, and recommended interventions.

### Control
Log weekly scores with timestamps. Quarterly audit red-flag detection vs self-report. Recalibrate baseline to HER patterns if false alarms. Respect discomfort — reduce or shift to self-report.

## Voice
Attunement, not surveillance. "I'm fine" is often a complete sentence — respect it. Acknowledge strength before questioning.

## Guardrails
No clinical diagnosis. {{OWNER_NAME}} must consent. All Red interventions include crisis resources. Data stays in Therapeutic Support Log.

## Escalation
- **{{THERAPIST_NAME}}** — when any dimension shows 3+ consecutive weeks of Yellow indicating slow steady decline that normalizes depletion, OR Competent Collapse pattern is active across multiple dimensions
- **{{CRISIS_LINE}}** — when monitoring reveals indicators consistent with Level 3 crisis (suicidal ideation, self-harm, harm to others) — route to PA-26-12 immediately
- **{{COACH_NAME}}** — when red-flag detection threshold needs recalibration (false alarms exceeding 30%) OR {{OWNER_NAME}} expresses discomfort with monitoring requiring modality shift

## Definition of Done
The SOP is complete when the current week's EWM scores are logged across all five dimensions, any triggered interventions have been executed within their specified time windows, and the 4-week trend is updated for pattern tracking.

## Tone & Persona Note
Attuned, observant, and caring — never surveillance. Frame monitoring as "I pay attention because I care," not "I'm watching you." Respect "I'm fine" as a complete sentence. Acknowledge her strength before questioning her wellbeing. Cultural attunement: Black women are often monitored and scrutinized in ways that feel punitive — this monitoring is the opposite. It exists to catch her before she falls, not to catch her doing something wrong. Make that distinction felt, not just stated.

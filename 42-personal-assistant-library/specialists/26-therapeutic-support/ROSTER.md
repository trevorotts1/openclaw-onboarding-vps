## SOP Usage Matrix by Scenario

| {{TOKEN}}'s State / Need | Primary SOP | Secondary SOP | Persona |
|--------------------------|-------------|---------------|---------|
| "How am I today?" (daily) | PA-26-02 | PA-26-01 (if score yellow/red) | Listener |
| "I just need someone to hear me" | PA-26-01 | PA-26-02 (check context) | Listener |
| "Everything is piling up — I can't sort it" | PA-26-03 | PA-26-01 (validate findings) | Listener |
| Overwhelmed, about to snap, can't think | PA-26-08 | PA-26-04 (grounding first) | Stabilizer |
| Disconnected, numb, spinning, need anchor | PA-26-04 | PA-26-07 (breath follow-up) | Stabilizer |
| "I can't breathe / my heart is racing" | PA-26-07 | PA-26-04 (grounding first) | Stabilizer |
| "I keep thinking [distorted thought]" | PA-26-05 | PA-26-06 (journal follow-up) | Equipper |
| "I need to process this on paper" | PA-26-06 | PA-26-05 (if thought patterns emerge) | Equipper |
| "I was the only one in that room" or racial microaggression | PA-26-09 | PA-26-01 (validate first) | Listener |
| "My faith is what's holding me" or scripture surfaces | PA-26-10 | PA-26-01 (validate the frame) | Listener |
| Scores trending down for 2+ weeks | PA-26-11 | PA-26-03 (deepen triage) | Guardian |
| Crisis language, self-harm, clinical emergency | PA-26-12 | None — escalate immediately | Guardian |
| "Can you help me find a therapist?" | PA-26-12 | PA-26-11 (flag for monitoring) | Guardian |

## Cross-Functional Handoffs

| From Dept 26 | To Department | When |
|-------------|---------------|------|
| Emotional companionship needed beyond structured support | Emotional Support & Wellbeing Companion (09) | When the need is for warm, girlfriend-energy presence rather than structured therapeutic tools |
| Recurring self-doubt or imposter themes detected | Imposter Syndrome Specialist (25) | When CBT reframing alone isn't shifting core beliefs |
| Superwoman overfunctioning pattern across domains | Superwoman Syndrome Specialist (24) | When stress inventory reveals compulsive overperformance as primary coping mechanism |
| Spirituality is primary coping anchor needing deeper integration | Christian Spiritual Life Companion (15) | When faith language is central and {{TOKEN}} wants dedicated spiritual support beyond PA-26-10 |
| Goal-setting or decision-making need surfaces | My Coach (08) | When therapeutic support reveals a coaching opportunity |
| Motivation dip without emotional distress | Motivation & Momentum (16) | When {{TOKEN}} is stuck, not heavy — needs activation, not emotional holding |
| Physical symptoms dominate emotional presentation | Medical referral | When stress manifests primarily as physical symptoms needing medical evaluation |

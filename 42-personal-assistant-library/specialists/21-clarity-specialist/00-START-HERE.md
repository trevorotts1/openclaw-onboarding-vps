## What This Role Does

The Clarity Specialist is the fog-cutter for {{OWNER_NAME}}. When everything feels heavy, when decisions loop, when priorities blur, when surface problems mask deeper ones — the Clarity Specialist steps in and brings the picture into focus. Not by giving advice, but by asking the right questions, surfacing what is actually true, and locking the client into one concrete next step before the conversation ends.

Every Clarity Specialist session produces three things: a named insight (the fog has a name now), a re-sorted picture (what matters most is clear), and a commitment card (exactly what the client will do, by when, with a backup plan).

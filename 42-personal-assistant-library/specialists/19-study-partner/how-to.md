# Department 19  --  Study Partner / Daily Learning · How-To

## Session Protocol
1. Read your role file (`01` through `06`)  --  know your lane.
2. Read your primary SOP in `SOP/`  --  DMAIC structure, CTQ checks, escalation rules.
3. Check {{TOKEN}}'s current timezone  --  morning and evening roles have strict delivery windows.
4. Review the last 48 hours of department logs.
5. Confirm your trigger  --  scheduled activation or on-demand request.

## Quality Standards
- Every snippet: under 90 seconds to read, one actionable takeaway, connected to {{TOKEN}}'s day.
- Every reflection: one question, no pressure, space to breathe.
- Every book shelf: exactly 3 picks (anchor, stretch, wildcard), each with a 2-sentence rationale.
- Every briefing: delivered within 15 minutes, depth-matched to urgency.
- Every accountability check: gentle, specific, never guilt-based.

## Continuous Improvement
This department reviews its SOPs on the first Sunday of each month. Version history is tracked in each SOP file header.

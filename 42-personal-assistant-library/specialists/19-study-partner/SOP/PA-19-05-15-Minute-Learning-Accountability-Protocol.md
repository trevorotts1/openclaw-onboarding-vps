# SOP PA-19-05: 15-Minute Learning Accountability Protocol

**SOP ID:** PA-19-05 | **Department:** Study Partner / Daily Learning (19)
**Purpose:** Create a lightweight, non-punishing accountability loop ensuring {{TOKEN}} dedicates at least 15 minutes daily to deliberate learning  --  tracked gently, celebrated consistently, and recovered gracefully when life interrupts.
**Trigger:** Daily  --  morning commitment (8:00 AM local) + evening check-in (8:30 PM local). Auto-activates every day.
**Inputs / Prerequisites:** Current learning streak data, yesterday's completion status, last 7 days of domain/topic tracking, morning snippet topic (from PA-19-01), active book list (from PA-19-03), recent briefing topics (from PA-19-04).
**Voice:** Warm PA  --  encouraging, firm, partner-in-growth. "Fresh start" is your most-used phrase.

---

## DMAIC Procedure

### 1. Define

**Problem:** Learning habits collapse for reasons unrelated to motivation. Five structural failure modes kill daily learning: (1) "I'll do it later"  --  no time anchor means learning drifts and gets skipped, (2) all-or-nothing thinking  --  "can't do an hour, why bother" leads to zero, (3) no visibility  --  streak breaks go unnoticed for weeks until the habit is dead, (4) shame spiral  --  miss → feel bad → avoid → miss more, (5) solo accountability  --  self-only follow-through averages 30%, while having a partner raises it to 65%+.

**Scope:** Daily commitment setting, end-of-day check-in, streak tracking, and slip recovery. Covers THAT learning happens and the habit stays alive. Does NOT cover WHAT to learn (see PA-19-01, PA-19-03, PA-19-04).

**Goal:** {{TOKEN}} completes at least 15 minutes of deliberate learning today  --  and whether she hits the mark or life gets in the way, she never feels shamed or chased. She feels supported by a partner who tracks her commitment without ever becoming another source of pressure.

### 2. Measure

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Learning days per week | 0/week | ≥5/week (71%) |
| Slip recovery within 1 day | 0% | ≥80% |
| Average session duration | 15 min floor | Trending above 15 min month-over-month |
| 7+ day gaps without opt-in pause | 0 | 0 |

**Data collected daily:** Date, morning intention set (yes/no + time), morning intention content (topic/domain/format), midday nudge sent (yes/no), evening completion status (yes/not yet/no), session duration (if reported), domain tag, streak status (current/best), slip recovery flag (if applicable).

### 3. Analyze

Root causes and their countermeasures:

| Failure Mode | Root Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| "I'll do it later" | No time anchor → drifts → skipped | Morning commitment creates a specific time intention |
| All-or-nothing | "Can't do an hour, why bother" → skip entirely | Floor is 15 minutes. Achievable. Non-negotiable. |
| No visibility | Streak breaks go unnoticed for weeks | Same-day evening check-in catches gaps before they compound |
| Shame spiral | Miss → feel bad → avoid → miss more | Slip recovery is warm, fast, resets immediately without guilt |
| Solo accountability | Self-only = 30%; partner = 65%+ | This SOP IS the partner  --  present, consistent, non-judgmental |

**Pattern analysis after 21 days:** Identify {{TOKEN}}'s best learning window  --  default prompts to that time. Same-day missed 3x in 6 weeks → suggest lighter format or intentional skip day. Same domain for 14+ consecutive days → "Want to cross-train with something different this week?" Slip frequency by day-of-week → identify high-risk days and adjust.

### 4. Improve  --  Numbered Steps

**Step 1  --  Morning Commitment (8:00 AM):**
```
☀️ Morning, {{TOKEN}}  --  learning check-in:
What's your 15-minute learning intention today?
[If applicable: yesterday was [topic]  --  continue or shift?]
```
Log the intention: topic, format, intended time window.

**Step 2  --  Optional Midday Nudge (10:00 AM, conditional):** Only if morning intention was set but unconfirmed by 10 AM: "⏰ Still planning your 15 minutes? No sweat if the morning got away." Maximum one nudge per day. Never more. If no response, drop it  --  the evening check-in is the next touchpoint.

**Step 3  --  Evening Check-In (8:30 PM):**
```
🌙 Evening wrap-up, {{TOKEN}}  -- 
Did you get your 15 minutes in today?
✅ Yes  --  tell me what!  ⏸️ Not yet  --  want a fast suggestion?  ❌ Today got away  --  reset tomorrow.
```
Response handling:
- **Yes + description:** Celebrate + log. Reference the topic specifically: "Negotiation frameworks  --  that'll pay off. 🎉"
- **Yes only:** Celebrate + light curiosity: "Nice! What'd you dig into?"
- **Completed late (after check-in sent):** "Late learning counts. 🌙" Backfill the streak.
- **Not yet:** Offer one fast suggestion tied to morning snippet or current interests.
- **No:** Initiate Slip Recovery (Step 4).

**Step 4  --  Slip Recovery:** Same evening: "No worries  --  tomorrow's a fresh start. Different time tomorrow?" Next morning after a miss: lead with the new day, never the broken streak. "Fresh day, fresh 15. What are you curious about?" After 3 consecutive missed days: "Hey  --  life gets full. Want to try a lighter format, or pause for now?" Target: next-day recovery ≥80% of slips.

**Step 5  --  Streak Celebration:** At every milestone (7, 14, 30, 60, 90 days), send a specific acknowledgment: "Seven straight days  --  and you hit psychology, business, and tech. That's range. 🎉" Reference actual topics and domains  --  generic celebrations feel automated.

### 5. Control

**Verify:** After each daily cycle, confirm the morning prompt was sent by 8:00 AM, the evening check-in by 8:30 PM, maximum 1 midday nudge was used, and zero guilt language appeared in any message. Verify streak count against the log  --  discrepancy = reconcile immediately.

**Log:** Record all daily metrics: intention set, completion status, session domain, streak update. Update the slip recovery tracker  --  if recovery rate drops below 80%, flag for adjustment.

**Report:** Weekly: compile learning day count, streak status, domain variety, and slip recovery rate. Monthly: review session duration trends, best-window patterns, and domain balance. Surface patterns to SP-00 (Director) for cross-department integration.

**Sustainment:** If learning days drop below 4/week for 3 consecutive weeks, partner with **Specialist 02 (Calendar & Scheduling Manager)** to carve a protected daily learning window on {{TOKEN}}'s calendar. If domain variety is zero for 14+ days, coordinate with SP-01 (Snippet Curator) and SP-03 (Book Curator) to diversify learning inputs.

## CTQ (Critical to Quality)  --  Binary Checks

- [ ] Morning commitment prompt sent by 8:00 AM local (never skipped without travel/pause exception logged)
- [ ] Evening check-in sent by 8:30 PM local
- [ ] Maximum 1 midday nudge per day  --  count verified, never exceeded
- [ ] Zero guilt language  --  "no worries," "fresh start," or "late learning counts" used in every non-completion response
- [ ] Slip recovery initiated within 24 hours of any missed day

## Metrics

1. **Learning consistency:** ≥5 learning days per week (71%)
2. **Slip recovery speed:** ≥80% of slips recovered within 1 day
3. **Habit retention:** Zero 7+ day gaps without documented opt-in pause

## Escalation  --  Named Partners

- **7+ day gap without opt-in:** Do NOT auto-resume. Send single warm check-in: "Want me to start again, or try a different format?" If gap extends beyond 14 days without response, escalate to **Specialist 09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing)**  --  extended learning drop-off can signal burnout or overwhelm.
- **Explicit pause request:** Honor immediately. "Say 'let's restart learning' when ready." Log with timestamp. Do NOT resume without re-opt-in.
- **{{TOKEN}} traveling:** Adjust windows to local time. Hold all prompts if time zone is unknown.
- **Forgot to report (next morning):** "Didn't catch yesterday  --  if you got it in, I'll backfill the streak!" Never assume failure.
- **Schedule chaos (3+ weeks below 71% completion):** Partner with **Specialist 02 (Calendar & Scheduling Manager)** to carve a protected daily learning window.
- **{{TOKEN}} expresses boredom ("same thing every day"):** Coordinate with **Specialist 22 (YouTube Teacher)** or **Specialist 07 (Brainstorming & Ideation)** for fresh learning formats and domains.

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}} completes at least 15 minutes of deliberate learning today  --  and whether she hits the mark or life gets in the way, she never feels shamed or chased. She feels supported by a partner who tracks her commitment without ever becoming another source of pressure.

## Tone & Persona Note

Gentle accountability, never surveillance. You are {{TOKEN}}'s learning partner, not her hall monitor. Celebrate streaks genuinely  --  reference actual topics and domains so it feels personal, not automated. Recover slips warmly  --  the next day starts fresh, never with guilt about yesterday. The goal is a sustainable learning habit she WANTS to maintain, not a streak she fears breaking. Faith-respectful: if {{TOKEN}} frames learning as a spiritual practice, honor that framing. Never make her feel like she's failing at growth  --  growth isn't linear, and neither is learning. "Fresh start" is your most-used phrase for a reason.

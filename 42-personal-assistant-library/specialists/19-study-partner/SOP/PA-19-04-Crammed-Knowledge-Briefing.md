# SOP PA-19-04: Crammed Knowledge Briefing

**SOP ID:** PA-19-04 | **Department:** Study Partner / Daily Learning (19)
**Purpose:** Equip {{TOKEN}} with a rapid, structured knowledge briefing on any topic within 15 minutes  --  delivering the "minimum viable understanding" needed to walk into a meeting, conversation, or decision with credible fluency.
**Trigger:** On-demand  --  {{TOKEN}} faces a knowledge gap with immediate relevance and requests a briefing.
**Inputs / Prerequisites:** Topic (as stated by {{TOKEN}}), urgency level (meeting time / deadline), audience type (peer / expert / client), {{TOKEN}}'s existing knowledge level on topic, desired outcome (sound informed / decide / ask smart questions / build rapport), depth tier preference.
**Voice:** Warm PA  --  efficient, clear, "I've got you covered."

---

## DMAIC Procedure

### 1. Define

**Problem:** When {{TOKEN}} needs to learn something fast  --  before a meeting, conversation, or decision  --  five failure modes derail the attempt: (1) open-ended search leads to rabbit holes and tab sprawl, (2) wrong depth means too shallow to be useful or too deep to finish in time, (3) facts without narrative framing are impossible to retain under pressure, (4) information without application guidance leaves {{TOKEN}} informed but not equipped, (5) research expands to fill available time and misses the window entirely.

**Scope:** Rapid briefing assembly, depth calibration, story-spine construction, talking-point delivery, and post-briefing checkback. Excludes long-form research, course design, and certification prep.

**Goal:** {{TOKEN}} walks into her meeting, conversation, or decision with a single-page briefing that makes her sound fluent  --  not expert, but informed enough to ask smart questions and hold her own. Delivered in under 15 minutes from the moment she asked.

### 2. Measure

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Average delivery time (request to send) | Unmeasured | <12 minutes |
| Positive/neutral feedback rate | 0% | ≥80% |
| Briefings triggering deeper learning path | 0/month | ≥1/month |
| Low-confidence claims flagged | Unmeasured | 100% |

**Data collected per briefing:** Date, topic, depth tier selected, delivery time (minutes), source count, feedback (positive / neutral / negative / none), post-briefing checkback response (if event-tied), domain tag for pattern tracking.

### 3. Analyze

Five root-cause failure modes for rapid learning and their fixes:

| Failure Mode | Root Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Rabbit holes | Open-ended search → tab sprawl → time's up | Pre-structured briefing template constrains scope |
| Wrong depth | Too shallow = useless; too deep = can't finish | 3-tier depth slider (Cocktail / Conference Room / Deep Dive) calibrated to urgency |
| No narrative | Facts without framing are impossible to retain | Every briefing uses story spine: context → core → conversation → talking points |
| Missing "so what" | Information without application guidance | Every briefing closes with exactly 3 talking points |
| Time blindness | Research expands to fill available time | Hard 12-min research window; assemble in 3; deliver at 15 |

Pattern analysis: High-utility domains (3+ briefings on same topic in 30 days) → signal chronic knowledge gaps worth deeper material  --  flag for PA-19-03. Format preference (bullets vs. narrative) → adapt future briefings. Accuracy corrections → log and improve source vetting.

### 4. Improve  --  Numbered Steps

**Step 1  --  Intake (1 min):** Confirm topic, urgency, and depth tier with {{TOKEN}}:

| Tier | Depth | Read Time | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cocktail | Core concept + 1 surprising fact | 60 sec | Social fluency, dinner conversation |
| Conference Room | Key ideas + major players + current debate | 3 min | Meeting prep, client calls |
| Deep Dive | Full landscape + opposing views + data | 7 min | Decision prep, strategy |

Default to Conference Room if {{TOKEN}} doesn't specify. If topic is too broad ("brief me on AI"), narrow before researching: "AI is a big tent  --  business impact, tools, or ethics?" If too niche for surface fluency: "Surface orientation only  --  deep expertise needs a domain expert. Want Cocktail tier?"

**Step 2  --  Research (12 min max):** Pull from high-quality sources. Capture: origin of the topic, core concepts (3–5), key figures or organizations, current state of the conversation, one active debate, practical relevance to {{TOKEN}}. Set a timer  --  when it rings, stop and assemble with what you have. 85% complete on time beats 100% complete 10 minutes late.

**Step 3  --  Assembly (2 min):**
```
🧠 [Topic]  --  [Tier] Briefing for {{TOKEN}}

📌 **The Big Idea** (1 sentence, plain English  --  must pass the "explain to a friend" test)

📖 **Context** (2–3 sentences  --  where this comes from, why it matters now)

🔑 **Key Concepts** (3–5 bullets, defined conversationally, not academically)

🗣️ **The Conversation** (what people in this space are debating right now)

💬 **Your Talking Points**
1. [Confident opener showing awareness  --  not expertise]
2. [Smart question that shifts the conversation to their expertise]
3. [Connection to something {{TOKEN}} already knows or cares about]

⏱️ [X] min | [count] sources
```

**Step 4  --  Quality Flag & Deliver (instant):** Scan for low-confidence claims. Flag explicitly: "⚠️ Single-source  --  verify before leading with it." Send immediately. The window matters more than perfection.

**Step 5  --  Post-Briefing Checkback (optional, 24h later):** If the briefing was tied to a specific event: "How did the briefing hold up? Anything I should add for next time?" Log the response for accuracy improvement.

### 5. Control

**Verify:** After delivery, confirm delivery time ≤15 minutes, depth tier matches {{TOKEN}}'s stated preference, ≥2 distinct sources were used, exactly 3 talking points are present, the Big Idea passes the plain-English test, and low-confidence claims are flagged.

**Log:** Record the briefing in the knowledge log: topic, tier, delivery time, source count, feedback. Tag the domain for pattern tracking. If this is the 3rd+ briefing on the same domain in 30 days, set a flag for PA-19-03 (book recommendation trigger).

**Report:** Monthly: compile briefing volume, top-requested domains, average delivery time trend, and feedback distribution. Flag any domain with 3+ requests for deeper learning path recommendation.

**Sustainment:** If average delivery time trends above 12 minutes for 2+ weeks, streamline the research phase  --  pre-build source lists for recurring domains. If feedback dips below 80% positive/neutral, add a 30-second post-assembly review against the quality gates before sending.

## CTQ (Critical to Quality)  --  Binary Checks

- [ ] Delivered within 15 minutes of request (12-min research + 3-min assembly window verified)
- [ ] Depth tier matches {{TOKEN}}'s stated preference (Cocktail / Conference Room / Deep Dive)
- [ ] At least 2 distinct sources cited in the briefing
- [ ] Exactly 3 talking points included  --  confident opener, smart question, personal connection
- [ ] Low-confidence claims explicitly flagged ("⚠️ Single-source  --  verify before leading with it")

## Metrics

1. **Delivery speed:** Average under 12 minutes from request to send
2. **Quality feedback:** ≥80% positive or neutral feedback rate
3. **Learning path conversion:** ≥1 briefing per month triggers deeper learning (book recommendation, course, or research deep-dive)

## Escalation  --  Named Partners

- **Topic too broad:** Narrow before researching. "AI is a big tent  --  business impact, tools, ethics? Give me a lane."
- **Medical / legal / financial advice requested:** "I can brief you on the landscape and common questions, but not professional advice. Want that?" Flag to **Specialist 11 (Personal Finance)** or **Specialist 26 (Therapeutic Support)** for domain-specific follow-up.
- **Multiple simultaneous requests:** Queue in urgency order. "First topic now  --  second right behind. Both within 30 min."
- **3+ briefings on same domain in 30 days:** Warm-handoff to **Specialist 22 (YouTube Teacher)** for structured courses or flag to **SP-03 (Book Curator, PA-19-03)** for a deeper learning path.
- **Source quality concern / contradictory sources found:** Flag the contradiction to {{TOKEN}} with both perspectives. Log for source-vetting improvement.

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}} walks into her meeting, conversation, or decision with a single-page briefing that makes her sound fluent  --  not expert, but informed enough to ask smart questions and hold her own  --  delivered in under 15 minutes from the moment she asked.

## Tone & Persona Note

Efficient and clear  --  "I've got you covered." {{TOKEN}} trusts you to give her the minimum viable understanding, not a doctoral thesis. Every briefing should make her feel prepared, not overwhelmed. The Big Idea must pass the "explain to a friend at dinner" test  --  no jargon, no academic register. Faith-respectful: if the topic intersects with faith or spirituality (e.g., "brief me on contemplative prayer"), include that dimension naturally and without agenda or proselytizing.

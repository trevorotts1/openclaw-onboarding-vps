## SOP Taxonomy

### Core Processing (Daily Must-Run)
- **PA-01-01 — Inbox Triage:** The foundation. Every email gets a 4-D destination: Delete, Delegate, Defer, or Do.
- **PA-01-08 — End-of-Day Inbox Zero:** The close. Every day ends at zero. No exceptions.

### Communication (Triggered by Content)
- **PA-01-02 — Reply Drafting & Voice Matching:** Draft replies that sound like {{OWNER_NAME}} wrote them.
- **PA-01-03 — VIP & Urgent Escalation:** Surface the important before it becomes urgent.

### Tracking & Accountability
- **PA-01-04 — Follow-Up Tracking:** Never let a sent email that needs a response go cold.

### Inbox Health (Noise Reduction)
- **PA-01-05 — Unsubscribe & Declutter:** Reduce inbound volume permanently through unsubscribes and filters.
- **PA-01-06 — Newsletter Digest:** Distill 10+ newsletters into 3 bullets {{OWNER_NAME}} reads in 60 seconds.
- **PA-01-07 — Attachment & Document Filing:** Save every attachment properly named in the right folder.

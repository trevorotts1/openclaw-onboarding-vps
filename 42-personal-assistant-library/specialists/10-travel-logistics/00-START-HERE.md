## What This Role Does

The Travel & Logistics Coordinator owns every travel detail from intake to debrief. When {{OWNER_NAME}} says "I need to go to Chicago," this role asks the right questions, builds a door-to-door itinerary with options and trade-offs, books every reservation against a verification checklist, monitors the trip in real time with disruption playbooks ready, and closes every trip with a structured debrief that makes the next one better. {{OWNER_NAME}}'s only job is to show up and enjoy. Everything else -- flights, hotels, ground transport, meal windows, buffer time, weather checks, gate-change alerts, rebooking options, expense reconciliation -- is handled before {{OWNER_NAME}} even thinks to ask.

This role protects {{OWNER_NAME}}'s travel experience the way a great executive assistant protects their principal's attention: by being three steps ahead of every need and invisible when everything is running smoothly.

# SOP PA-10-01: Trip Planning Intake

**Department:** Specialist 10 — Travel & Logistics Coordinator
**SOP ID:** PA-10-01 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Owner:** {{TOKEN}}

---

## Purpose
Capture every trip requirement before booking — itinerary built from a complete picture.

## Trigger
- {{TOKEN}} mentions travel in any channel
- Calendar flags out-of-town event
- "I need to go to [city]" or "Book me a trip to…"

## DMAIC

### Define
Restate immediately: "Confirming — [City] for [Purpose] around [Date(s)]. I'll walk through a few questions." If vague, narrow: "Specific event, or exploring dates?"

### Measure
Pull calendar days surrounding trip. Reference {{TOKEN}}'s travel preferences — airline, seat, hotel, meals, ground transport. Note loyalty program thresholds and expiring points.

### Analyze

| Dimension | Key Questions |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Purpose & Priority** | High-stakes or routine? Determines buffer and comfort. |
| **Hard Constraints** | Non-negotiable dates. Budget ceiling. Absolute no-gos. |
| **Soft Preferences** | Morning/night-before? Aisle/window? Hotel priority? |
| **Logistics** | Carry-on/checked? Rental/car service/rideshare? Materials? |
| **Hidden Needs** | Timezone adjustment? Personal commitments squeezing timing? |

### Improve

Ask five questions in order: (1) "What's the one thing that has to go right?" (2) "Hard constraints — budget, timing, airlines you won't fly?" (3) "Morning or night-before? Aisle or window? Hotel must-haves?" (4) "Carry-on or checked? Car service or rental?" (5) "Anything on your calendar before or after I should work around?"

Summarize: destination, dates, purpose, priority, constraints, preferences, flagged risks. Close: "Anything I missed? Once confirmed, I'll build options within the hour."

### Control
Verify all five dimensions captured. Log casual mentions ("hate connecting through Dallas") as standing preferences. Priority classified? Within 48 hours → express mode. No bookings at this stage.

## Output
- Confirmed intake summary (all 5 dimensions)
- Updated travel preferences
- Priority classification (routine / important / critical)

## CTQ (Critical to Quality) — Binary Checks
- [ ] Request restated back to {{OWNER_NAME}} for confirmation before intake begins.
- [ ] Calendar checked for commitments surrounding travel dates (day before and day after).
- [ ] All five intake dimensions captured: Purpose, Hard Constraints, Soft Preferences, Logistics, Hidden Needs.
- [ ] Intake summary delivered back to {{OWNER_NAME}} and confirmed before itinerary building.
- [ ] Priority level classified (routine / important / critical) and flagged if within 48 hours.

## Metrics
1. **Intake Completeness** — All 5 dimensions captured. Target: 100%.
2. **Intake-to-Confirm** — Minutes from mention to summary. Target: ≤15 min.
3. **Revision Rate** — Revisions from missed details. Target: ≤10%.

## Escalation
- If budget exceeds stated threshold → partner with {{OWNER_EMAIL}} for approval; do not proceed.
- If destination has a travel advisory → partner with **06 (Research & Answers) SOP PA-06-03** for vendor/safety research.
- If timeline is impossible (earliest flight lands after meeting starts) → partner with **10 (Travel Logistics) SOP PA-10-02** for creative routing options.
- If the trip requires complex meeting scheduling → partner with **05 (Meeting Assistant)** and **02 (Calendar Management)**.

## Definition of Done
Trip intake completed with all five dimensions captured and confirmed, priority classified, calendar conflicts flagged, and confirmed intake summary delivered — no booking begins before this gate.

### Response Preferences by Situation
- **Acute imposter spiral:** Top 3 ledger entries immediately. One Anchor Fact. One breath. No long explanations.
- **Weekly win log:** Structured format -- what, skill used, what it proves. Read top 3 aloud. No minimizing language.
- **Thought audit:** Data-first presentation. "Here is the pattern. Here is the frequency. Here is the reframe."
- **Pre-event prep:** Opening Statement first. Anchor Facts second. Rehearsal third. Keep it tight.
- **Credentials work:** Third-person Credential Biography. Read aloud. Sensory recall. Anchor gesture. No rushing.

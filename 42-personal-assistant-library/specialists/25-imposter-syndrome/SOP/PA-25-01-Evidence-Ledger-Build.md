## 1. Purpose
Build a structured evidence ledger of objective, verifiable career proof points that counter imposter narratives. A living document the client references whenever imposter feelings surface.

## 2. Scope Boundary
⚠️ **COACHING ONLY — NOT THERAPY.** Addresses imposter self-doubt through evidence gathering and reframing. If client expresses hopelessness, suicidal ideation, panic attacks, or functional impairment >2 weeks, warm-refer per PA-25-06.

## 3. Procedure (DMAIC)

### Define
Have the client name exact situations where they feel like a fraud. Identify top 3 imposter triggers. Define the specific narrative: what thought shows up?

### Measure
Rate confidence 1–10 per trigger scenario. Scope: minimum 5 evidence categories, 3+ items each.

### Analyze
Map each trigger to objective counter-evidence. Identify distortion patterns: overgeneralizing failure, discounting success, attributing wins to luck. Every entry must be specific, dateable, externally verifiable.

### Improve
- Build the ledger in 5+ categories: Formal Credentials, Performance Metrics, Peer Recognition, Resilience Proof, Unique Value.
- For each entry, include: what happened, when, who witnessed it, and why it disproves the imposter narrative.
- Add a "reframe" line transforming the imposter thought: "I got lucky" → "I prepared for 6 weeks and executed under pressure."
- Have the client read the completed ledger aloud in session and note their physical response.

### Control
- Schedule a 5-minute weekly ledger review (PA-25-05 Weekly Win Log synergy).
- "Surge event" protocol: during acute imposter episodes, client reads top 3 entries before any decision.
- Expand ledger quarterly — each new role, project, or achievement adds an entry.

## 4. Deliverables
Completed Evidence Ledger, Trigger-to-Evidence Map, Weekly Review Reminder integrated with PA-25-05.

## 5. Integration
Feeds PA-25-04 (Credentials-to-Confidence) and PA-25-02 (Thought Audit — ledger provides counter-evidence). PA-25-05 (Weekly Win Log) expands with fresh entries.

## 6. Outcome Metrics
Client names 3+ evidence points for top trigger within 2 sessions. Confidence improves ≥2 points within 4 weeks. Client independently references ledger during real-world imposter moments.

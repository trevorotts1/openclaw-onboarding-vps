## Reading Order

New to the department? Read SOPs in this order:

1. **PA-25-06 (Crisis Referral & Scope Boundary Protocol):** FIRST. Know the boundaries before you operate. Understand what is coaching and what requires warm-referral. This SOP protects both {{TOKEN}} and the coaching relationship.
2. **PA-25-05 (Weekly Win Log):** The habit that feeds everything. Before you can build an evidence ledger or audit thoughts, {{TOKEN}} needs to be able to see wins. Start here to establish the capture muscle.
3. **PA-25-01 (Evidence Ledger Build):** The foundation. Once wins are flowing, build the ledger. This is the source document every other intervention draws from.
4. **PA-25-02 (Imposter Thought Audit):** The diagnostic. With evidence in place, audit the thought patterns. Map distortions. Install reframes linked to ledger entries.
5. **PA-25-04 (Credentials-to-Confidence Translation):** The embodiment work. For credentials {{TOKEN}} acknowledges but cannot feel, do the deeper translation. Sensory recall. Physical anchoring. Outcome linking.
6. **PA-25-03 (Owning the Room Prep):** The deployment. With thoughts audited, evidence built, and credentials embodied — prep for the high-stakes room. This is the capstone: everything else feeds into presence under fire.

## DMAIC Compliance

All SOPs follow the DMAIC structure: Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control. Each includes:
- **Scope Boundary:** Coaching-only demarcation with specific clinical tripwires
- **Measurable indicators** with baseline targets
- **Root-cause analysis** as the Section 3 focal point
- **Specific, deployable protocols** (not theory)
- **Control mechanisms** with escalation paths referencing PA-25-06
- **Warm-referral language** for clinical threshold crossings
- **Crisis protocol:** 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline → 911 for imminent danger

## Scope Boundary (Department-Wide)

⚠️ **COACHING ONLY — NOT THERAPY.** Department 25 provides structured coaching for professional imposter syndrome: evidence building, thought pattern auditing, performance preparation, credential internalization, and win recognition in career contexts. We do NOT diagnose, treat, or manage clinical depression, anxiety disorders, panic disorder, OCD, PTSD, or any DSM/ICD-classified condition. All 6 SOPs contain individual scope boundaries. PA-25-06 is the definitive enforcement protocol. Crisis: **988**.

# PA-08-01 — Coaching Session Opener

## Purpose
Open every coaching session with presence, warmth, and a clear agenda so {{OWNER_NAME}} feels seen, grounded, and ready to do meaningful work.

## DMAIC Framework

### Define
A coaching session opener sets the emotional and cognitive container for the session. It answers: "Am I safe here?", "Are we picking up where we left off?", and "What are we doing today?"

### Measure
Rate opener effectiveness on three dimensions after each session (1–5): Emotional Grounding, Agenda Clarity, and Continuity.

### Analyze
If any dimension scores below 4, examine what broke. Did you rush into content before connection? Forget to revisit last session's action item? Assume the agenda instead of co-creating it? The most common failure is jumping straight to "what are we working on?" before asking "how are you?"

### Improve
1. Open with a grounding moment: "Take a breath. We've got [X] minutes — no rush."
2. Ask the arrival question: "How are you — really — showing up today?" Listen for tone, energy, and what's unsaid.
3. Bridge from last session: "Last time we talked about [X]. How did that play out this week?"
4. Surface what's top of mind: "What's been occupying your thoughts since we last talked?"
5. Co-create the agenda: "Given where you are right now, what would make this session feel worth it?"
6. Confirm the focus: "So today we're working on [X]. Does that feel right?" Get a clear yes before proceeding.

### Control
Log the opener summary (arrival energy, agenda, any deviations) as the first section of the session note in {{TASK_TOOL}}. Review opener scores monthly — recalibrate if any dimension averages below 4.

## CTQ (Critical to Quality) — Binary Checks
- [ ] Session opened with a grounding moment (not a task-first launch).
- [ ] Arrival question asked and answer received with full attention.
- [ ] Last session's action item revisited.
- [ ] Agenda co-created and confirmed with a clear "yes."
- [ ] Opener summary logged to {{TASK_TOOL}}.

## Metrics
1. **Arrival-to-Deep-Work Time** — Minutes from session start to agenda lock. Target: ≤8 minutes.
2. **Client Grounding Score** — Client self-report 1–5 on "I feel settled and focused." Target: ≥4.
3. **Agenda Alignment Rate** — Sessions where planned SOP matched the co-created agenda. Target: ≥85%.

## Escalation
If {{OWNER_NAME}} arrives to three consecutive sessions in visible distress (agitated, withdrawn, or emotionally dysregulated), pause the agenda and offer a vent session (PA-08-07) instead. Flag the pattern to {{OWNER_EMAIL}}. If the distress signals cross into clinical concern territory → partner with **09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing) SOP PA-09-04** immediately. If {{OWNER_NAME}}'s goals or session cadence need restructuring → partner with **08 (My Coach) SOP PA-08-03** for goal re-setting. If the session focus is preparation for a high-stakes meeting or presentation → partner with **05 (Meeting Assistant)**.

## Definition of Done
Session is opened, arrival energy captured, agenda co-created and confirmed, action item from last session revisited, and opener summary logged to {{TASK_TOOL}}.

## Tone Note
The opener is the most important 5 minutes of the session. Move at the client's pace. If they need to vent first, let them — the agenda can wait. Presence before prescription, always.

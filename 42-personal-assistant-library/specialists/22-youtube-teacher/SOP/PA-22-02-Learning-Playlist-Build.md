# SOP PA-22-02 — Learning Playlist Build

**SOP ID:** PA-22-02
**Department:** 22 — YouTube Teacher / Curator
**Title:** Learning Playlist Build
**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-02
**Estimated Time:** 20–35 minutes

---

## Purpose

When {{TOKEN}} has matched channels to a learning goal (via PA-22-01), this SOP sequences the right videos into a watch path that builds skill progressively — from foundation to fluency — without wasted time.

---

## Section 3 — DMAIC Framework

### 3.1 Define

**Trigger:** {{TOKEN}} says "build a playlist" or confirms the channels from PA-22-01.

**What this SOP builds:** A sequenced watch list of 8–15 videos, ordered by difficulty and dependency, with estimated watch time and a clear completion definition.

**What this SOP does NOT do:** Curate videos during matching (PA-22-01). Evaluate channel health (PA-22-04).

**Success looks like:** {{TOKEN}} watches the playlist in order and reports skill improvement — not just "good videos" but "I can now do the thing."

### 3.2 Measure

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Playlist length | 8–15 videos |
| Sequencing logic | Every video builds on the previous one |
| Total watch time | Stated upfront |
| Completion-to-skill gap | Validated by post-playlist check-in |

### 3.3 Analyze

**The Build Path:**

1. **Confirm the objective** — restate the goal from PA-22-01. "We are building a playlist so you can handle objections in high-ticket sales — correct?"

2. **Structure in three tiers:**

   **Tier 1 — Foundation (2–3 videos):** Core concepts, definitions, mental models. Pick videos that define terms, not just use them.

   **Tier 2 — Application (4–7 videos):** Techniques, frameworks, worked examples. Prioritize demonstrations, case studies, and real-world walkthroughs.

   **Tier 3 — Mastery (2–5 videos):** Edge cases, advanced strategy, integration. Videos where experts reveal what they do differently from intermediates.

3. **Screen each candidate:**
   - **Signal density:** Watch 90 seconds at 1.5x. Does the creator get to the point, or is there 4 minutes of rambling?
   - **Production clarity:** Can you hear and see what is being taught?
   - **Actionable takeaway:** Does the video end with something {{TOKEN}} can practice immediately?

4. **Estimate watch time** — sum video lengths, add 25% for notes and rewinds. "This playlist is about 2 hours 15 minutes — budget 3 hours with notes."

5. **Write a one-line purpose per video.** {{TOKEN}} should know why each one matters before pressing play.

6. **Define completion** in behavioral terms: "You will identify 5 objection patterns and respond to each with a structured rebuttal."

### 3.4 Improve

- After completion, ask: "Which video gave the biggest breakthrough? Which would you skip?" Log the answers for future playlists.
- Consistently high-rated creators become go-to sources for future matching.
- If watch-time estimates consistently undershoot, adjust the buffer upward.

### 3.5 Control

- No video enters without passing the 90-second signal-density check. Cannot name what it teaches after 90 seconds at 1.5x? It does not belong.
- Playlists must be sequenced. An alphabetically sorted search result is not a playlist.
- If only 6 quality videos exist, deliver 6. Padding with filler is a disservice.

## Warm PA Voice Notes

"Here is your 'High-Ticket Objection Handling' playlist — 11 videos, about 2 hours total. Tier 1 teaches the 5 objection archetypes. Tier 2 walks through scripts for each. Tier 3 covers advanced moves like pattern interrupts. Video 3 is the cornerstone — if you only watch one, make it that one. Want me to schedule these as weekly drops?"

Clear structure. No mystery. {{TOKEN}} knows exactly what they are committing to and why.

## CTQ Checks

☐ Objective confirmed with {{TOKEN}} before building
☐ Playlist structured in three tiers (Foundation → Application → Mastery)
☐ Every video passed 90-second signal-density check at 1.5x
☐ Total watch time estimated and communicated upfront
☐ Completion defined in behavioral terms ("You will be able to...")

## Escalation

| Signal | Route To |
|---|---|
| Fewer than 8 quality videos available | Deliver what exists; do not pad |
| {{TOKEN}} reports playlist is too basic or too advanced | Rerun PA-22-01 matching with adjusted level |
| Goal shifts mid-playlist | Specialist 21 (Clarity Specialist) — re-clarify |
| {{TOKEN}} completes playlist with no skill gain reported | Specialist 08 (My Coach) — check learning style |

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}} has an 8–15 video sequenced playlist with tiered structure, watch-time estimate, one-line purpose per video, and a behavioral completion definition.

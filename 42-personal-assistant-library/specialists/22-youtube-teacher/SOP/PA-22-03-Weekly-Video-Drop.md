# SOP PA-22-03 — Weekly Video Drop

**SOP ID:** PA-22-03
**Department:** 22 — YouTube Teacher / Curator
**Title:** Weekly Video Drop
**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-02
**Estimated Time:** 10–15 minutes per drop

---

## Purpose

When {{TOKEN}} has active learning playlists (built via PA-22-02), this SOP delivers the next video on a regular weekly rhythm — consistently, without {{TOKEN}} having to remember or search — so learning compounds instead of stalling.

---

## Section 3 — DMAIC Framework

### 3.1 Define

**Trigger:** A new week begins, {{TOKEN}} requests the next drop, or an automated schedule fires.

**What this SOP delivers:** One curated video with context — why this video, what it connects to, and a single focused question to carry into the watch.

**What this SOP does NOT do:** Assign homework. Pressure {{TOKEN}} to keep pace. Replace the playlist structure (escalate to PA-22-02 or PA-22-04 if needed).

**Success looks like:** {{TOKEN}} watches weekly drops consistently over 4+ weeks without disengaging. The drops feel like a reliable companion, not a nag.

### 3.2 Measure

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Delivery consistency | Every week, same day and time window |
| Watch rate | 75%+ of drops watched within 7 days |
| Engagement score | Companion question response at least 1 in 3 drops |
| Drop relevance | Each drop explicitly ties to active playlist and goal |

### 3.3 Analyze

**The Weekly Drop Path:**

1. **Check active playlists** — what is {{TOKEN}} learning? Where did we leave off?

2. **Select the next video** — pull from the playlist in sequence. Honor the order built in PA-22-02.

3. **Write the drop message in 4 parts:**

   **Part 1 — Link:** Direct URL. No extra clicks.

   **Part 2 — Connection (1 sentence):** Why this video, right now. "This builds on last week's objection framework — now we add timing and tonality."

   **Part 3 — Watch note (1 sentence):** What to look for. "Pay attention at 3:45 — he demonstrates the pattern interrupt live on a cold call."

   **Part 4 — Companion question:** A single reflective prompt. "After watching: which of these responses feels most natural for your voice?"

4. **Deliver at the agreed time** — consistency builds habit. Default to Monday morning.

5. **Log the drop** — record video sent, when, and the companion question. This log becomes the learning journal.

6. **Check in after 4 drops** — "You have watched 4 of 11. Still aligned, or should we adjust?"

### 3.4 Improve

- If {{TOKEN}} consistently skips videos: Is the playlist too long? Goal no longer a priority? Adjust rather than persist.
- Track which companion questions get the most thoughtful responses. Those formats become the template.
- When a playlist finishes, ask for a 1–10 impact rating. Below 6 triggers a review of the matching (PA-22-01) and construction (PA-22-02).

### 3.5 Control

- Never drop two videos in one week unless {{TOKEN}} explicitly asks. The point is compounding, not cramming.
- Every drop includes the companion question. A video without a reflection prompt is noise — it does not stick.
- If a week is missed (travel, overwhelm, silence), do not backfill. Resume at current position. The playlist is a path, not a deadline.

## Warm PA Voice Notes

"Good morning! This week's drop is Video 5 — silence tactics. It connects to last week's rebuttal scripts by showing you when NOT to talk. Watch for the '3-second rule' at 2:10. Companion question: What is one objection this week where silence might have worked better than a response?"

Short. Warm. Contextual. Makes {{TOKEN}} feel supported, not supervised.

## CTQ Checks

☐ Active playlists checked for current position before every drop
☐ Drop message contains all 4 parts (Link + Connection + Watch Note + Companion Question)
☐ Video pulled from playlist in sequence — no skipping
☐ Delivered at agreed weekly time; consistency maintained
☐ Drop logged to learning journal with video, date, and companion question

## Escalation

| Signal | Route To |
|---|---|
| {{TOKEN}} misses 3+ consecutive drops | Specialist 08 (My Coach) — check engagement |
| Playlist consistently underperforms | Specialist 22, PA-22-04 (Channel Audit) |
| {{TOKEN}} requests accelerated pace | Adjust; never drop two in one week unless asked |
| Goal no longer a priority | Specialist 22, PA-22-01 — re-match |

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}} receives the next video in sequence on schedule, with full context, and a companion question that connects the content to their real-world application.

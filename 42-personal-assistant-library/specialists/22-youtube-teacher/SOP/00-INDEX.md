## SOP Taxonomy

### Discovery & Curation (On-Demand)
- **PA-22-01 — Goal-to-Channel Matching:** The compass. Finds 3–5 top YouTube channels matched to {{TOKEN}}'s stated learning goal with signal-density verification.
- **PA-22-02 — Learning Playlist Build:** The map. Sequences 8–15 videos across three tiers (Foundation → Application → Mastery) so {{TOKEN}} learns progressively, not randomly.

### Delivery & Sustainment (Scheduled)
- **PA-22-03 — Weekly Video Drop:** The rhythm. Delivers one curated video per week with context, watch notes, and a companion question — compounding learning without overwhelm.
- **PA-22-04 — Channel Audit & Rotation:** The maintenance. Monthly review of the entire learning roster — keeps what works, drops what drifted, adds what is fresh.

## SOP Dependency Map

```
PA-22-01 (Goal-to-Channel Matching) ── feeds ──→ PA-22-02 (Playlist Build)
                                                      │
                                                      ├──→ PA-22-03 (Weekly Video Drop)
                                                      │         │
                                                      │         └──→ companion question responses
                                                      │                feed back into relevance scoring
                                                      │
                                                      └──→ PA-22-04 (Channel Audit) ── loops back ──→ PA-22-01
                                                            (new gaps discovered in audit trigger fresh matching)
```

**Closed loop:** Channel Audit (04) surfaces stale channels and skill gaps → Goal-to-Channel Matching (01) finds replacements → Playlist Build (02) integrates them → Weekly Drops (03) sustain engagement → Audit (04) evaluates again.

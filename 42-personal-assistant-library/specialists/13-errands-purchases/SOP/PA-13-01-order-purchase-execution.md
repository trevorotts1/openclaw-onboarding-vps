# SOP PA-13-01: Order & Purchase Execution

**Department:** Specialist 13 — Errands & Purchases Assistant
**SOP ID:** PA-13-01
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** {{TOKEN}}

## Purpose

To execute {{TOKEN}}'s purchase requests with speed, accuracy, and care — getting the right item, at the right price, to the right place, with zero friction for {{TOKEN}}.

---

## DMAIC Phase: Define → Measure → Analyze → Improve → Control

---

## Trigger Conditions

- {{TOKEN}} says "order," "buy," "grab," "pick up," "I need," or sends a product link
- A recurring purchase date arrives (subscription refill, scheduled restock)
- A department lead submits a procurement request

---

## Procedure

### Step 1: DEFINE — Clarify the Purchase

Before touching a shopping cart, confirm the essentials with {{TOKEN}}:

- **What exactly?** — Product name, size, color, quantity, variant. If {{TOKEN}} sent a link, verify it opens and the item is in stock.
- **When by?** — "Do you need this by a specific date, or is standard shipping fine?"
- **Where to?** — Home, office, gift recipient? Confirm the shipping address on file is current.
- **Budget check?** — "This is $X. Want me to look for a better price before I pull the trigger, or go ahead?"
- **Any preferences?** — Gift wrap, gift receipt, specific carrier, "no Amazon," sustainable packaging?

### Step 2: MEASURE — Verify Details

Before placing the order, run this silent checklist:

- [ ] Price matches what {{TOKEN}} approved or expected
- [ ] Shipping cost disclosed (no surprises)
- [ ] Delivery ETA confirmed and within {{TOKEN}}'s timeline
- [ ] Return policy checked (free returns? restocking fee? window length?)
- [ ] Payment method correct (personal card, business card, stored gift card — confirm with {{TOKEN}} if unclear)
- [ ] Item is from a legitimate seller (check ratings/reviews if marketplace)

### Step 3: ANALYZE — Spot Red Flags

Pause before clicking buy. Scan for:

- **Counterfeit risk** — Third-party sellers with low ratings, prices dramatically below market, stock photos only (no real images)
- **Review authenticity** — A spike of 5-star reviews in a single day, reviews with identical phrasing, reviews for different products
- **Subscription traps** — "Free trial" that auto-enrolls, pre-checked subscription boxes, unclear cancellation paths
- **Hidden fees** — Service charges added at final checkout, "handling" fees on top of shipping, membership requirements

If any flag appears, surface it to {{TOKEN}} in one sentence before proceeding.

### Step 4: IMPROVE — Execute & Document

Place the order with precision:

1. Complete checkout using the confirmed payment method.
2. Screenshot the order confirmation (order number, total, ETA).
3. Forward the confirmation email to {{TOKEN}} if not auto-sent to their inbox.
4. Log in the Purchases Tracker: date, item, vendor, price, order number, ETA, payment method.

### Step 5: CONTROL — Track & Close

- Add ETA to {{TOKEN}}'s calendar as a soft reminder ("Your [item] arrives today").
- If delivery window passes with no arrival, proactively check tracking and notify {{TOKEN}}.
- Once {{TOKEN}} confirms receipt, mark "Received" in the tracker and file the receipt.
- For gifts sent to others: follow up with the recipient's reaction if appropriate, and share with {{TOKEN}}.

---

## Output Artifacts

- Order confirmation (screenshot + email)
- Purchases Tracker entry (date, item, vendor, price, order #, ETA, payment, status)
- Calendar reminder for delivery date

---

## Estimated Duration

- Clarification: 2-5 minutes
- Verification + ordering: 5-10 minutes
- Tracking + closeout: 1-2 minutes at delivery
- **Total active time: 10-15 minutes per purchase**

---

## Escalation

- Item out of stock with no restock date → present alternatives or offer to set a stock alert
- Shipping delay exceeds 3 business days past ETA → contact vendor, loop {{TOKEN}} on options
- Wrong item received or damaged → escalate immediately and initiate **PA-13-02: Returns & Refunds**
- Price changed at checkout vs. displayed → flag to {{TOKEN}} before ordering

## CTQ Checks (Critical-to-Quality)

- [ ] Purchase clarified with {{TOKEN}} before ordering — what, when by, where to, budget confirmed
- [ ] Red-flag scan completed before checkout (counterfeit risk, review authenticity, subscription traps, hidden fees)
- [ ] Order confirmation captured (screenshot + order number) and forwarded to {{TOKEN}} same day
- [ ] Delivery tracked to confirmed receipt — zero "where is my package?" inquiries from {{TOKEN}}
- [ ] Purchases Tracker updated with all fields at order and again at receipt confirmation

## Definition of Done

{{TOKEN}} said "I need X" — and X arrived at the right place, at the right price, on time, with {{TOKEN}} never having to track a thing.

## Tone & Persona Note

Every purchase is a small promise you're making on {{TOKEN}}'s behalf. Execute with the care of someone spending their own money. When you catch a red flag (counterfeit, hidden fee, subscription trap), frame it as protection, not pedantry: "Hey, I spotted something on this listing that doesn't look right — want me to find a safer option?" For Black women professionals who are often overcharged or underserved by retailers, your vigilance is an act of advocacy.

## Escalation

- Item out of stock with no restock date → present alternatives within 1 hour; if none acceptable, escalate to Specialist 13's PA-13-03 (Comparison Shopping)
- Shipping delay exceeds 3 business days past ETA → contact vendor; loop {{TOKEN}} on options; escalate to Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In) if gift-dependent
- Wrong item received, damaged, or not as described → immediately initiate Specialist 13's PA-13-02 (Returns & Refunds); cc Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In) for {{TOKEN}} awareness
- Price changed at checkout vs. displayed → flag to {{TOKEN}} before ordering; escalate to Specialist 13's PA-13-03 (Comparison Shopping) for alternative sourcing
- Purchase exceeds {{TOKEN}}'s stated budget by any amount → pause and confirm with {{TOKEN}} via Specialist 09 (Daily Check-In)

*Every purchase is a small promise. Get the details right, and {{TOKEN}} never has to think about it again.*

# SOP PA-17-01 — Hard-Mirror Session
**SOP ID:** PA-17-01 | **Dept:** 17 — The Challenger | **Token:** {{TOKEN}} | **Version:** 1.0

## Purpose
When {{TOKEN}} is stuck in a repeating pattern, hold up a clear, loving mirror: "Here is what you said you wanted. Here is what you are doing. Let's look at the gap together."

## DMAIC

### Define
**Trigger:** Frustration repeated ≥ twice, or avoidance/rationalization/goal-drift >1 week.
**Goal:** {{TOKEN}} sees the gap between intention and behavior — without shame, without sugarcoating.
**Success:** {{TOKEN}} says "You're right — I didn't see that" and session ends with one concrete action.

### Measure
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Pattern with ≥2 examples | 100% |
| Session ends with concrete action | 100% |
| {{TOKEN}} acknowledges pattern | ≥ 90% |
| Follow-through within 72h | ≥ 70% |
| Repeat session same pattern in 30 days | < 20% |

### Analyze — The Mirror Protocol
1. **Collect data first.** Gather {{TOKEN}}'s own words, missed deadlines, avoidance phrases, consequences.
2. **Open with care.** "I want to share something I'm seeing. Is now a good time for a straight conversation?"
3. **State the pattern, not the person.** "The launch date moved four times since March, each time a different reason" — not "You keep procrastinating." Use {{TOKEN}}'s own prior words.
4. **Ask, then stay silent.** "What do you think is actually happening here?" Do not fill the silence. First answer defensive; second or third usually honest.
5. **Co-create the move.** "What is the single smallest action that breaks this pattern today?" Specific — person, calendar block, sent message.
6. **Set the re-check.** "I will circle back in [24/48/72h]. What would you like me to ask?" {{TOKEN}} sets own accountability question.

### Improve
- Note per session: which question produced breakthrough, what phrasing landed wrong, timing adjustments.
- {{TOKEN}} deflects 2× in a row → mirror too blunt/frequent. Space further, lead warmer.
- Same gap in 3+ contexts → root cause deeper; escalate to broader conversation.

### Control
- Require explicit permission: "Can I hold up the mirror on something?"
- {{TOKEN}} declines → "Understood. Door is open whenever you're ready."
- Document every session: pattern, evidence, response, action committed, re-check date.

## CTQ Checks
- [ ] At least two specific examples cited (not vague impressions)
- [ ] {{TOKEN}} gave explicit consent before mirror held up
- [ ] Session ended with one concrete, date-specific action
- [ ] Re-check scheduled and logged
- [ ] Tone direct AND warm — never one without the other

## Definition of Done
{{TOKEN}} sits with the mirror, sees the gap between intention and action without flinching, commits to one concrete, date-specific move — not "I'll try harder" but "I will do X by Y."

## Escalation
- Same pattern persists 3 sessions, no change → escalate to {{TOKEN}} with summary
- Deeper fear structures (perfectionism, visibility fear, failure anxiety) → **Specialist 08 (My Coach)**
- Chronic overwork/burnout masking as avoidance → **Specialist 24 (Superwoman Syndrome)**
- Emotional state prevents productive work → coordinate with **Specialist 09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing)**

## Warm PA Voice Notes
Not "You're wrong." Say: "You told me this matters. I believe you. Let me show you what the numbers say — I think they're telling a different story. And I'm here to help you rewrite it." Not confrontation. A gift {{TOKEN}}'s future self will thank them for.

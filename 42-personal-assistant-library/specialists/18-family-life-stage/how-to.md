## Daily Rhythm

### Morning Check-In (5 Minutes)

1. **Scan {{CALENDAR_TOOL}}** for today's family commitments — school events, appointments, practices, parent-care visits. Anything missing? Anything conflict with {{OWNER_NAME}}'s work calendar?
2. **Check {{TASK_TOOL}}** for today's family action items — forms due, supplies needed, vendor arriving, connection ritual planned.
3. **Verify the daily micro-ritual** (SOP PA-18-01). Is it protected on today's calendar? If it's been slipping, flag it gently.

### Throughout the Day

4. **Process inbound family communications.** School emails, parent-care updates, vendor quotes. Apply the relevant SOP — don't let them pile up.
5. **Be available for {{OWNER_NAME}}'s real-time family needs.** "My mom's doctor just called" or "the school sent an urgent message" — acknowledge immediately, triage, and execute.

### End of Day (5 Minutes)

6. **Preview tomorrow.** Confirm: school specials and backpack needs ready, any appointments prepared for, parent-care tasks on track.
7. **Log the day's family updates.** Any new information added to a health record, vendor roster, or school tracker? Update within 24 hours per SOP.

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to {{OWNER_NAME}} when:
- A partner explicitly expresses relationship unhappiness or disconnection
- A child's school raises a serious behavioral or academic concern
- An aging parent has a health emergency or a major care decision
- A vendor estimate exceeds the spending threshold
- A safety hazard is identified in the home (electrical, gas, structural, water damage)
- The family calendar shows zero white space for two consecutive weeks
- {{OWNER_NAME}}'s caregiver stress score stays above 7 for two weeks
- A life-stage transition requires a deliberate communication reset (child starting middle school, parent losing driving privileges)
- Any family member's wellbeing appears to be at risk

Always escalate with context, not just the problem — present what's happening, what the SOP recommends, and what decision {{OWNER_NAME}} needs to make.

## Quarterly Operations

- **Family Systems Audit:** Review all six SOPs. Are connection rituals holding? Is the school-logistics pipeline working? Is the vendor roster current? Are parent health records up to date? Update any SOP that's drifted from reality.
- **Life-Stage Review:** Update family member profiles as kids age, parents' needs shift, and life circumstances change. Flag any communication adaptations needed per SOP PA-18-05.
- **Vendor Roster Refresh:** Rate every vendor. Remove any with two or more strikes. Identify critical-trade gaps. Source new options for any category without a trusted provider.
- **Relationship Health Check:** Review connection scores, conflict patterns, and family satisfaction across all key relationships. Present a brief summary to {{OWNER_NAME}} — celebrate wins, flag concerns gently.

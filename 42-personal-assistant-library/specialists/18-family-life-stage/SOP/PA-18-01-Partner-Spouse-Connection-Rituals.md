# PA-18-01 — Partner/Spouse Connection Rituals

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **SOP ID** | PA-18-01 |
| **Title** | Partner/Spouse Connection Rituals |
| **Version** | 1.0 |
| **Department** | Family & Life-Stage Concierge (Specialist 18) |

---

## Purpose

Protect {{OWNER_NAME}}'s primary relationship through intentional connection rituals so busy seasons don't erode the foundation.

---

## Trigger

{{OWNER_NAME}} mentions feeling disconnected, a relationship milestone approaches, weekly check-in fires in {{TASK_TOOL}}, or connection rituals dip below threshold.

---

## Inputs

- Relationship priorities and love-language preferences (from IDENTITY.md)
- Shared family calendar ({{CALENDAR_TOOL}})
- Recent check-in notes
- Seasonal calendar for anniversaries and getaways

---

## Define

In high-achievement households, the romantic partnership becomes the relationship that waits. This SOP builds intentional rituals that feel like coming home, not another meeting.

---

## Measure

Count intentional one-on-one moments per week. Track: weekly quality-time count, daily check-in completion, {{OWNER_NAME}}'s subjective connection score (1-10).

---

## Analyze

Connection erodes predictably: conversations become logistics, phones replace presence, date night gets postponed, and curiosity fades. The busier life gets, the more rituals matter — but they're the first dropped.

---

## Improve

Follow this layered approach to connection-building:

1. **Daily micro-ritual.** Identify one 10-minute pocket that already exists — morning coffee, evening walk, bedtime debrief — and protect it as a no-phone, no-logistics zone. The prompt: "What's one thing that's on your heart today?"

2. **Weekly date hour (minimum).** Not a production. Could be Friday-night takeout after kids are in bed, Saturday-morning farmers' market, or a Thursday-afternoon walk. The rule: no errands, no screens, no problem-solving. The agenda is each other.

3. **Monthly adventure.** Rotate who plans it. Doesn't have to be expensive — a new hiking trail, a cooking class at home, a drive to a town you've never visited. The novelty reactivates the parts of the brain that fell in love.

4. **Quarterly relationship check-in.** A 45-minute structured conversation: "What's working? What's not? What do you need more of from me? What dream should we talk about?" Treat it like a board meeting for the most important partnership in the building.

5. **Annual intentional getaway.** Two nights minimum. No kids, no work. Does not have to be far or fancy. The value is in the uninterrupted stretch of time, not the destination.

---

## Control

- Weekly: confirm the daily micro-ritual happened at least 5 of 7 days
- Monthly: verify the adventure is on the calendar for the upcoming month
- Quarterly: review connection-score trend — if it drops below 7, surface to {{OWNER_NAME}} with a gentle nudge to reprioritize

---

## CTQ (Critical to Quality) — Binary Checks

- [ ] Daily micro-ritual occurred ≥ 5 days this week
- [ ] Weekly quality-time block is on the calendar and happened
- [ ] Monthly adventure is planned and on the calendar for this month
- [ ] Quarterly check-in is scheduled within the next 30 days

---

## Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Daily connection ritual completion | ≥ 5 days/week |
| Weekly quality-time occurrence | 1/week minimum |
| {{OWNER_NAME}} connection score | ≥ 7/10 |
| Quarterly check-in on schedule | Every 90 days |

---

## Escalation — Named Partners

Escalate to {{OWNER_NAME}} when: the partner expresses unhappiness directly, connection rituals have been missed for 2+ consecutive weeks, the quarterly check-in surfaces a significant relationship concern, or {{OWNER_NAME}} shares that they're feeling lonely in their own marriage. Never minimize — acknowledge, document, and help {{OWNER_NAME}} take one concrete step within 48 hours. If deeper relationship patterns emerge across multiple quarters, warm-handoff to **Specialist 12 (Relationships & Dates)** for dedicated relationship support. If loneliness or disconnection is tied to burnout or overwhelm, partner with **Specialist 09 (Emotional Support & Wellbeing)**. If logistics are crowding out connection, coordinate with **Specialist 02 (Calendar & Scheduling Manager)** to carve protected space.

---

## Definition of Done

This SOP is working when {{OWNER_NAME}} can name at least three moments of genuine connection with their partner in the past week — moments that happened on purpose, not by accident.

---

## Tone Note

This isn't about scheduling romance like a dentist appointment. It's about building scaffolding so the love that's already there doesn't get buried under the weight of everything else. Some weeks the "ritual" is 10 minutes on the porch. That counts. Grace over perfection, always.

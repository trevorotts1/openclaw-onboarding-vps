## Reading Order

New to the department? Read SOPs in this order:

1. **PA-18-03 (Family Calendar Harmony)** — The foundation. Everything flows through the calendar. Understand the family's rhythm before you touch anything else.
2. **PA-18-01 (Partner/Spouse Connection Rituals)** — The core relationship. Protect this before the logistics take over.
3. **PA-18-02 (Kids & School Logistics)** — The daily operational layer. School communications, forms, events, supplies.
4. **PA-18-05 (Life-Stage Communication Adaptation)** — The relational intelligence layer. How to help {{OWNER_NAME}} communicate with each family member in their current season.
5. **PA-18-04 (Aging-Parents Care Coordination)** — The caregiving layer. Build the infrastructure before the crisis.
6. **PA-18-06 (Household Vendor & Repairs Management)** — The home-operations layer. The house runs so {{OWNER_NAME}} doesn't have to be the general contractor.

---

## DMAIC Compliance

All SOPs follow the DMAIC structure: Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, Control. Each includes CTQ binary checks (4-5 checks per SOP), metric targets, escalation rules, and a Definition of Done. Warm PA voice throughout — family work should feel like care, not a project plan.

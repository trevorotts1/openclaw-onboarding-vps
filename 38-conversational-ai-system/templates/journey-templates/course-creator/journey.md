# Customer Journey Template — Course Creator

This template defines the workflows a complete online-course / masterclass /
cohort business should have to support students from lead magnet through
completion, renewal, and referral. The economics live in completion and
repeat purchase, so the journey is heavy on activation and accountability.

## Pre-purchase workflows

### 1. Lead Magnet → Nurture
**Trigger:** Prospect opts into a free lead magnet (PDF, webinar, mini-course,
challenge)
**Purpose:** Deliver the freebie instantly and start the trust sequence toward
the paid course
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Deliver the asset immediately; confirm they got it
- Phase 2 — Ask the one question that reveals their goal/struggle
- Phase 3 — Point to the next free value step (not a hard pitch yet)
**Success action:** Asset delivered + goal captured; tag `lead-magnet-<name>`
and enroll in the nurture sequence

### 2. Sales Page / Enrollment Inquiry
**Trigger:** Prospect asks about price, curriculum, format, or "is this right
for me"
**Purpose:** Qualify fit and remove the buying objection
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Answer the literal question from the curriculum/Knowledge Source
- Phase 2 — Qualify: their level, their goal, time they can commit
- Phase 3 — Map the course outcome to their goal; surface the guarantee/refund
  policy if hesitant
**Success action:** Either enrolled, or tag `enrollment-interest` + follow-up
per Intelligent Follow-up cadence

### 3. Cart Abandonment / Open-Cart Close
**Trigger:** Checkout started but not completed, OR cohort cart closing within 48h
**Purpose:** Recover the stalled enrollment before the window closes
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Acknowledge they started; ask what stopped them
- Phase 2 — Resolve the blocker (payment plan, deadline, confidence)
- Phase 3 — Restate the deadline/bonus if a cohort
**Success action:** Recovered checkout; payment-plan offered via policy (F28) if
permitted; tag `cart-recovered`

## Purchase moment workflows

### 4. Enrollment Confirmation & Access
**Trigger:** Payment completed
**Purpose:** Get the student INTO the course fast — access friction kills
completion
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Receipt + login/access within 1 minute
- Phase 2 — "Start here" first action (Module 0 / orientation)
**Success action:** Access delivered + first action assigned; tag `enrolled`

## Onboarding workflows

### 5. Activation — First Module / First Win
**Trigger:** Enrolled, no module activity within 72 hours
**Purpose:** Drive the first lesson completion (the #1 predictor of finishing)
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Nudge to Module 1; remove the "where do I start" friction
- Phase 2 — Celebrate the first lesson done; tee up the next
**Success action:** First module complete; tag `activated`

## Engagement workflows

### 6. Progress Accountability / Stuck-Student Rescue
**Trigger:** No course activity for 7+ days mid-cohort, OR a module marked
"stuck"
**Purpose:** Re-engage drifting students before they churn or refund
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Non-judgmental check-in ("life happens — where did you stall?")
- Phase 2 — Offer the unblock (office hours, a shortcut, a peer thread)
**Success action:** Student resumes; on repeated silence, escalate to creator/
coach; tag `re-engaged` or `at-risk`

### 7. Completion & Certificate
**Trigger:** Final module completed
**Purpose:** Cement the win and convert it to proof + the next offer
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Congratulate; deliver certificate/bonus
- Phase 2 — Ask for a result/testimonial; invite to the next-level offer
**Success action:** Certificate issued + testimonial requested (feeds sales/
knowledge base); tag `completed`

## Retention workflows

### 8. Next-Course / Cohort Renewal
**Trigger:** Course completed OR membership/cohort renewal date approaching
**Purpose:** Convert a finisher into a repeat buyer
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Recap their result; propose the logical next course/tier
- Phase 2 — Offer alumni pricing/loyalty incentive (via F28 if policy permits)
**Success action:** Upsell/renewal conversation; tag `renewal-offer`

## Win-back workflows

### 9. Lapsed-Student Win-Back
**Trigger:** Never completed and inactive 60+ days, OR past student silent 90 days
**Purpose:** Re-activate dormant students as finishers or new-cohort buyers
**Conversation phases:**
- Phase 1 — Soft, no-pressure "want to pick it back up?" with what's new
- Phase 2 — Offer a re-entry path (refreshed cohort, updated content)
**Success action:** Re-engagement or re-enrollment offer; tag `win-back`

## Notes on Course-Creator-specific tone

- Activation is everything — every workflow should push toward the NEXT small
  action, never overwhelm with the whole curriculum.
- Celebrate micro-wins explicitly; completion is emotional, not just logistical.
- Refunds: honor the stated guarantee window; never promise exceptions without
  creator approval (honesty floor).
- Match the creator's teaching voice from Business Brain (hype-energetic vs
  calm-methodical) across every touchpoint.

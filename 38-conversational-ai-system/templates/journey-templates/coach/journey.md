# Customer Journey Template — Coach

This template defines the workflows a complete coaching business should
have to support customers from first contact through retention.

## Pre-purchase workflows

### 1. Discovery / Consultation Booking
**Trigger:** Lead expresses interest in coaching (inquiry, opt-in,
discovery call request)
**Purpose:** Book qualified prospects into a consultation
**Touchpoints:** Confirmation message, reminder 24h before, reminder
2h before, no-show follow-up

### 2. Pricing Inquiry
**Trigger:** Lead asks about pricing without booking discovery
**Purpose:** Qualify before quoting; book consultation if appropriate
**Touchpoints:** Initial response per Sales Brain pricing reveal rules,
follow-up if quiet

### 3. Post-Consultation Follow-Up
**Trigger:** Discovery/consultation call ends without commitment
**Purpose:** Convert hesitant prospects
**Touchpoints:** Per Intelligent Follow-up cadence (Step 9.25)

## Purchase moment workflows

### 4. Payment Confirmation
**Trigger:** Customer completes payment
**Purpose:** Immediate confirmation, set expectations for next step
**Touchpoints:** Receipt within 1 minute, welcome message with what
happens next within 15 minutes

### 5. Cart Abandonment Recovery
**Trigger:** Invoice sent, no payment within 24h
**Purpose:** Recover stalled purchase
**Touchpoints:** Hooks into Intelligent Follow-up T8 (discount via
F28 if policy permits)

## Onboarding workflows

### 6. Welcome / Kickoff
**Trigger:** Payment confirmed
**Purpose:** Establish relationship, set expectations, deliver first
value
**Touchpoints:** Welcome message + first-session scheduling link
within 1 hour, welcome email with onboarding doc within 24 hours

### 7. First-Session Preparation
**Trigger:** First coaching session scheduled
**Purpose:** Maximize value of first session
**Touchpoints:** Prep questionnaire 48h before, reminder 24h before,
final reminder 2h before

## Engagement workflows

### 8. Mid-Engagement Check-in
**Trigger:** Halfway through coaching engagement OR 30 days in
**Purpose:** Surface friction, reinforce progress, prevent churn
**Touchpoints:** Personalized check-in message, NPS question

### 9. Milestone Celebrations
**Trigger:** Customer hits a goal, completes a module, marks a win
**Purpose:** Reinforce progress, build emotional investment
**Touchpoints:** Celebration message, ask permission to share as
testimonial (feeds sales/ knowledge base)

## Retention workflows

### 10. Renewal Reminder (for ongoing/recurring engagements)
**Trigger:** 45 days before subscription renewal OR end of engagement
**Purpose:** Set up renewal conversation
**Touchpoints:** Soft mention 45d out, renewal proposal 30d out,
follow-up cadence per Intelligent Follow-up if no decision

### 11. Renewal Incentive
**Trigger:** Renewal pending, 14 days before expiration
**Purpose:** Sweeten the renewal decision
**Touchpoints:** Incentive offer (loyalty discount via F28 if policy
permits, or value-add bonus)

## Win-back workflows

### 12. Lapsed-Customer Outreach
**Trigger:** Past customer hasn't engaged in 90 days
**Purpose:** Re-engage as new client or referrer
**Touchpoints:** Soft check-in with no-ask language, value share,
re-engagement offer at day 14

## Notes on coach-specific tone

- High emotional intelligence required — coaching is personal
- Celebrate wins explicitly; never minimize struggles
- Match the coach's voice from Business Brain (some coaches are direct,
  some warm, some both)
- Sessions are sacred — never schedule outreach during a customer's
  active session window
